<section begin="Summary" />'''The Moomin Storybook''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Moomin]] who offers Quests and [[Moomin Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Moomin Storybook is found resting on a stone pedestal at the center of [[Moominvalley Glade]]. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|necklace_u|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|outfit_u|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|prop_u|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|prop_m|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders. This Ultimate Guide also provides new cosmetics and an [[Expression]] in their Quest Tree, permanently available to all players: an {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|outfit_i|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|prop_p|inline_text|label=Moomin Family Painting Prop}}, and the {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|emote|inline_text}} Expression.<section end="Summary" /><!--<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Moomin Guide offered 3 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Moomin only.

===<span id="Ultimate Outfit">Moomin Ultimate Outfit</span>===
The Moomin Ultimate [[Outfit]] is a peach knee-length dress with a white ruffled collar and a special effect that renders the player's limbs partially transparent, reflecting the colors of the surrounding environment. This Outfit closely resembles the dress [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/moominmamma/ Moominmamma] gifts to [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/ninny-the-invisible-child/ Ninny] upon her arrival to the Moominhouse. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Moomin}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fourth node. When combined with the {{Spirit Item|Proud Victor|Mask|inline_text|label=Proud Victor Mask}}, {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Neckpiece|inline_text|label=Sense of Self Neck Accessory}}, and the {{Icon|Voice_basic_call.png|inline_text|link=Base Call|label=Base Call}}, the player's voice is modified to a chiming bell.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Umbrella">Moomin Ultimate Umbrella</span>===
The Moomin Ultimate [[Prop|Umbrella]] is red with a black handle that's decorated with white diamonds and Moomin silhouettes. When equipped, this umbrella is held by the player and provides protection from the [[Flora, Minerals and Elements of Sky#Rain|rain]]. It cannot be placed and therefore cannot be used in [[Shared Spaces]]. This Prop closely resembles the umbrella carried by [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/too-ticky/ Too-Ticky] while guiding [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/ninny-the-invisible-child/ Ninny] through the rain to the Moomintroll family in the [https://moomin.fandom.com/wiki/Moomin_(1990_TV_series) 1990s animated cartoon]. When carried on its owner's back, the Prop appears folded up in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Moomin}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Plush">Moomin Ultimate Plush</span>===
<!-- ::'''''"A brave companion for the adventures that await you."''''' -->
The Moomin Ultimate [[Prop|Plush]] is a stuffed toy shaped like a sitting [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/moomintroll/ Moomintroll] that can be hugged and tossed around for other players to squeeze as well. It is a placeable [[Prop]] that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. When carried on its owner's back, the Prop appears in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item  during the Season, available to be purchased for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Moomin}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node.<br>

==Additional Collectibles==
Upon completing Quests from the Season of Moomin, additional collectibles are available to be collected for {{Candle}} [[Currency#Candles|regular Candles]] or {{Heart}} [[Currency#Hearts|Hearts]] through the Moomin Storybook's [[#Quest Tree|Quest Tree]]. No [[Season Pass]] or other [[IAP]] purchase is necessary for these collaboration items. '''All items WILL remain in the Ultimate Guide's Quest Tree after the Season ends.'''

===Outfit===
The [[Outfit]] offered by The Moomin Storybook is the [[Outfits#Base_Outfit|Base Outfit]] with a special effect that renders the player's limbs partially transparent, reflecting the colors of the surrounding environment, costing {{Cost|190 C}}. When combined with the {{Spirit Item|Proud Victor|Mask|inline_text|label=Proud Victor Mask}}, {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Neckpiece|inline_text|label=Sense of Self Neck Accessory}}, and the {{Icon|Voice_basic_call.png|inline_text|link=Base Call|label=Base Call}}, the player's voice is modified to a chiming bell. It is '''permanently available''' to all players after completing [[Season of Moomin#Quest #6|Quest #6]] of the Season of Moomin, from the Quest Tree's seventh node.<br>

===Prop===
The [[Prop]] offered by The Moomin Storybook is a large rectangular painting depicting the Moomintroll family and [https://www.moomin.com/en/characters/ninny-the-invisible-child/ Ninny] sitting together on a stone cliff facing the sea, framed by thick wood that's adorned by a small bundle of flowers, costing {{Cost|35 C}}. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It is '''permanently available''' to all players after completing [[Season of Moomin#Quest #6|Quest #6]] of the Season of Moomin, after unlocking the Quest Tree's sixth node.<br>

===Expression===
This Ultimate Guide offers the {{Emote|Moomin Guide|inline_text}} [[Expression]], which has 4 Levels.<br>
<br>
[[File:{{Icon Name|Moomin Guide|emote}}|x170px|left]]
<gallery heights="125" captionalign="center">
Moomin-Guide-read-emote-level-1.gif|{{Icon Name|Moomin Guide|emote_name}} Level 1
Moomin-Guide-read-emote-level-2.gif|{{Icon Name|Moomin Guide|emote_name}} Level 2
Moomin-Guide-read-emote-level-3.gif|{{Icon Name|Moomin Guide|emote_name}} Level 3
Moomin-Guide-read-emote-level-4.gif|{{Icon Name|Moomin Guide|emote_name}} Level 4
Moomin-Guide-read-emote-book.png|Book close-up
</gallery>

*Level 1 will make your avatar pull out a small book from behind their back and sit down with crossed legs to begin reading it, their head subtly moving side-to-side as they scan the pages. Your avatar will continue reading until you move; when you do, your avatar will close the book before standing up and putting away the book behind their back.
*Level 2 makes the same motion as Level 1, with a white halo over the book as your avatar pulls it out and sparkles swirling above the open book's pages as it is read, with a puff of dust appearing from the book when it is closed.
*Level 3 will make your avatar pull out a small book from behind their back and lay down on their belly with their feet raised and ankles crossed, their head subtly moving side-to-side as they scan the pages. Your avatar will continue reading until you move; when you do, your avatar will close the book before standing up and putting away the book behind their back.
*Level 4 makes the same motion as Level 3, with a light blue halo over the book as your avatar pulls it out and sparkles swirling above the open book's pages as it is read.

===Bell Call===
{{#ev:youtube|Q4KIU0uKoYY||right|}}
When players wear the {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|outfit_u|inline_text}} or the {{Spirit Item|Moomin Guide|outfit_i|inline_text}} offered by the [[The Moomin Storybook]] in combination with the {{Spirit Item|Proud Victor|Mask|inline_text|label=Proud Victor Mask}}, {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Neckpiece|inline_text|label=Sense of Self Neck Accessory}}, and the {{Icon|Voice_basic_call.png|inline_text|link=Base Call|label=Base Call}}, the player's voice is modified to a chiming bell. Unlike regular [[calls]], the Bell Call '''cannot''' be accessed through the Expression Menu.

==Quest Tree==
