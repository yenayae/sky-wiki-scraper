The '''Season of Lightseekers''' began on September 23, 2019 and ended on November 10, 2019, lasting a total of 48 days. It was the second seasonal event in Sky since its global launch.

The Season of Lightseekers succeeded the [[Season of Gratitude]] and preceded the [[Season of Belonging]].

==Welcome==
<center class="title-text-welcome">[https://thatgamecompany.com/welcome-season-lightseekers/ Welcome to the Season of Lightseekers!]</center>

Six Spirits themed off [[Hidden Forest]] have arrived in Sky, offering new unique expressions and cosmetics. Look through the six realms of Sky with your friends and relive the spirits' memories!

A number of changes have been introduced with this season:

*A new small area in [[Isle of Dawn]]
*Seasonal Spirits offer new expression types
**Expressions you can use with friends
**Stances you can use to change how you stand
**Calls you can use to change your 'Chirp'/'Voice'
*A new Daily Quest;'' 'Catch the Light' ''

==Daily Quests & Seasonal Candles==
===Daily Quests===
Collect Seasonal Candles throughout the Season to trade with the new spirits for their cosmetics. A total of 4 seasonal candles are available to gather, as follows:

* Each day the Lightseekers Guide stood next to a Realm's portal, indicating where the day's daily quests could be completed. Players would earn one seasonal candle per quest completed. There were 2 quests in total. Complete the quests and you would claim your reward by talking to the Lightseekers Guide (2 candles)
* Players could collect an extra candle by collecting the 4 seasonal bunches found in the realm the Lightseekers Guide was standing next to. <small>(more information on the "Seasonal bunches locations" below).</small> (1 candle)
* Players who had the [[Adventure Pass]] could claim a Seasonal Candle at Home as a free daily bonus. (1 candle)

===Seasonal Candle Bunches Locations ===
This season, the Seasonal Candle bunches are found in 1 realm per day, excluding [[Isle of Dawn]] and [[Eye of Eden]].

* [[Daylight Prairie]]: 4 around the [[Prairie Caves|Caves]] area
* [[Hidden Forest]]: 4 around the [[Forest's Brook]] area
* [[Valley of Triumph]]: 4 around the [[Ice Rink]] area
* [[Golden Wasteland]]: 4 in the [[Broken Temple]] area
* [[Vault of Knowledge]]: 4 around the [[First Floor Vault]]

==Spirits and Cosmetics==
All of the ''Season of Lightseekers'' Spirits can be found scattered around the 6 realms of Sky. There were 2 Expressions, 2 Friendship Expression, 1 Stance, and 1 Call to gather - overall, 6 spirits.

==Trivia==
*The candles for this season were blue, but this didn't carry on due to blue being hard to see in specific areas.
*The Isle Spirit, Piggyback Lightseeker, used to only be obtainable once the other 5 spirits were all found, as it was blocked by a constellation gate requiring 5 Lightseeker Spirits.
*The mask that the Lightseekers Guide wears is unobtainable. However, it is available to players in the [[NetEase/Cosmetics|Chinese version of Sky]].
* The Season of Lightseekers is depicted in several merch items from [[That Sky Shop]]. To know more about That Sky Shop products, visit their website: https://thatskyshop.com

==Bugs==
* When Daylight Savings Time ended, there was a bug that caused the four physical Seasonal Candles to reset everytime the area was reset, letting people be able to obtain more candles than they should.
