<section begin="Summary"/>The '''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Abyss]] who offers quests and [[Abyss Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Abyss Guide is found standing on the boat in the [[Treasure Reef]]. The Ultimate Guide offered an {{Spirit Item|Abyss Guide|necklace_u|link=#Abyss Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, an {{Spirit Item|Abyss Guide|mask_u|link=#Abyss Ultimate Mask|inline_text}}, an {{Spirit Item|Abyss Guide|headpiece_u|link=#Abyss Ultimate Head Accessory|inline_text}}, and an {{Spirit Item|Abyss Guide|cape_u|link=#Abyss Ultimate Cape|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders. This Ultimate Guide also provided a new cosmetic in their Quest Tree, permanently available to all players: a {{Spirit Item|Abyss Guide|mask|link=#Mask|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Abyss Guide offered 3 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Abyss only.

=== Abyss Ultimate Mask===
The Abyss Ultimate [[Mask]] is a beige mask that covers the bottom half of the player's face. At the top, it is decorated with small dark-brown triangles. The mask is hollow from the inside with a horizonal round-edged cylindrical shape, topped with a light turquoise stone in the middle. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Abyss}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node.

The mask gives you 15 more seconds of breathing when [[Season of Abyss#Dive_Mechanic|diving]].<br>

===Abyss Ultimate Head Accessory===
The Abyss Ultimate [[Head Accessory]] is a small braid on the right side of the players head, adorned with a bronzed hair ring with a turquoise rhombus-shaped stone in the middle. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Abyss}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node. <br>

===Abyss Ultimate Cape===
The Abyss Ultimate [[Cape]] is a red cape with a light- and dark-brown zig-zag border at the bottom and top hem. In the front, the two cape sides at the top are connected with bronzed rings and a cape chain, the chain itself being a turquoise color. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Abyss}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node. <br>

===Mask===
The [[Mask]] offered by the Abyss Guide consists out of a dark-grey [[Masks#Base Mask|Base Mask]], with a brown mask overneath that covers the bottom half of the face and is adorned with light-grey triangles along the top and a turquoise stone in the middle, costing {{Cost|48 C}}. The Mask offers players an additional 15 seconds of oxygen when underwater. It is permanently available to all players after completing Quest #5 of the Season of Abyss, from the Quest Tree's sixth node.<br>
