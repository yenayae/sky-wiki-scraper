This calculator was created by {{Userlinks|Ray808080}}. It will tell you the Seasonal Candles you've missed, whether or not you can buy all the Cosmetics during the Season, and how many days you cannot miss in order to get enough Seasonal Candles to buy all the Cosmetics. Everything should be displayed in your timezone.

You can find the [[MediaWiki:SkySeasonCandleCalculator.js|Source Code here]].

Alternatively, you use the [https://sky-season-calculator.netlify.app/ Sky Season Calculator by Stoat] which allows you to check off specific items, OR the [https://sky-planner.com/season-calculator Sky Planner's Season Calculator by Silverfeelin] which has additional features and makes the experience more visual.
