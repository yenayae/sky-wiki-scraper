<section begin="Summary" />The '''Comfort of Kindness''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Moomin]]. The Comfort of Kindness is found on a grassy mound to the right in [[Moominvalley Glade]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|Spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are a {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|Neckpiece|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|Hair|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|Cape|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|Prop_p|inline_text|label=Bridge Painting Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|Prop_c|inline_text|label=Chandelier Prop}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of two [[Props]]: a [[#Bridge Painting|Bridge Painting]] and a [[#Chandelier|Chandelier]].

====Bridge Painting====
The first [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is an oval painting depicting a bridge over a river with a thick wooden frame, decorated by three spikes on top and a single spike on the bottom<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|6 SC|season=Moomin}} total).<br>

====Chandelier====
The second [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a simple bronze chandelier with four arms, each holding a white candle<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and functions as a light source. Once placed, the player can light the candles and interact with the item to switch the light off and on. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|42 SC|season=Moomin}} total).<br>
