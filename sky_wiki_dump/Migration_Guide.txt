<section begin="Summary" />The '''Migration Guide''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Migration]] who offers Quests and [[Migration Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Migration Guide is found on the left side of [[Isle of Dawn]], at the Migration camp after the large hill at the start of the Realm. The Ultimate Guide offers a {{Spirit Item|Migration Guide|necklace_u|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Migration Guide|outfit_u|inline_text}}, {{Spirit Item|Migration Guide|shoes_u|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Migration Guide|facepiece_u|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders. <!--This Ultimate Guide also provides a new Prop in their Quest Tree, available to all players: a {{Spirit Item|Migration Guide|prop_u|inline_text}}.--><section end="Summary" /><br>

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Migration Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Migration Ultimate [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Migration offers 3 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Migration only.

===<span id="Ultimate Outfit">Migration Ultimate Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Spirit is a short-sleeved top with high, bulky collar, and a pair of shorts with leggings underneath. The top and shorts are teal and fur-lined. A blue, orange-red, and yellow cloth is tied around the waist, securing an additional fringed white piece. Golden bands decorate the lower legs. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased with {{Cost|2 SH|season=Migration}}.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Shoes">Migration Ultimate Shoes</span>===
The [[Shoes]] offered by this Spirit are thick fur-lined boots with a deep tongue. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased with {{Cost|1 SH|season=Migration}}.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Face accessory">Migration Ultimate Face Accessory</span>===
The [[Face Accessory]] offered by this Spirit is a wooden headpiece shaped like the snout of the Antlered Manatee. Two large antlers are secured to this with colorful strips of fabric. From the sides of the accessory are two pointed ribbons, one blue and one orange, hung from golden diamonds. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased with {{Cost|2 SH|season=Migration}}.<br>
