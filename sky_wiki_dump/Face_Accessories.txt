'''Face Accessory''' is a general term in Sky for any [[Cosmetics]] added to the player's face. This includes half-masks, glasses, and jaw accessories. Face Accessories share the second [[Outfit Shrine]] from the left with [[Masks]], where they are listed in the second tab (the sunglasses {{Spirit Item|Chill Sunbather|facepiece}} icon). One [[Mask]] and one Face Accessory can be worn simultaneously but both can be worn individually as well.  
{{clear|left}}

==Special Face Accessories==
In general, the only effect of changing your Face Accessory is the the appearance of your face. However, there are some Face Accessories with special functions.

* The [[Elder of the Isle#Ultimate Shield Mask|Isle]], [[Elder of the Prairie#Ultimate Shield Mask|Prairie]], and [[Elder of the Forest#Ultimate Shield Mask|Forest]] Elder Face Accessories obtained after completing the respective Elder's Constellation can grant the player protection from hazards such as Dark Dragons and Crabs. More details are listed on the Elders' pages.
* The [[Chill Sunbather#Mask|Chill Sunbather's Face Accessory]], [[Days of Feast#Feast Goggles|Feast Goggles]], [[Days of Nature#Nature Glasses Pack|Nature Glasses]], [[Days of Style#Style Heart Sunglasses|Style Heart Sunglasses]], [[Days of Style#Style Flame Sunglasses|Style Flame Sunglasses]] and [[Days of Style#Style Star Sunglasses|Style Star Sunglasses]] apply different tints to the game when activated.
