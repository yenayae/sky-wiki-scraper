This Spirit offers the {{Emote|Migrating Bellmaker|inline_text}}, which modifies the player's voice to a distorted, rippling voice.

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Hair">Hair</span>===
The [[Hair]] offered by this Spirit is a voluminous medium-length hair with a cloth headband and small side braid. The headband rests on the forehead and has a squared-off chevron pattern. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is offered to all players for {{Cost|23 SC|season=Season of Migration}} during the Season.<br>

The [[Head Accessory]] offered by this Spirit are a pair of round, brown glasses. The front of the arms have small diamond decorations. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season.<br>

===<span id="Cape">Cape</span>===
The [[Cape]] offered by this Spirit is a thick fur-trimmed cape with multi-colored segments. The orange-red underlayer has a wavy shape that droops at the front, hemmed in a yellow squared-off zig zag. The blue upper layer draws down at the neck, and forms a thick tail at the back. The fluffy collar is tipped in golden, bell-shaped ornaments. <!--description by Ozotronic-->It is offered to all players for {{Cost|36 SC|season=Season of Migration}} during the Season.<br>
