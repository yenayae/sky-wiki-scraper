<section begin="Summary" />The '''Radiance Guide''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Radiance]] who offers Quests and [[Radiance Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Radiance Guide is found in the Dye Workshop. The Ultimate Guide offers a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Guide|necklace_u|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Guide|cape_u|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Guide|mask_u|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Radiance Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Radiance only.

===<span id="Ultimate Mask">Radiance Ultimate Mask</span>===
The Radiance Ultimate [[Mask]] is an animal-inspired mask featuring a head with horns. A golden rim runs from the snout to the mask's flat semicircular ears, which feature dangling rectangular earrings. Thick markings outline the eyes. Attached to the head is a pair of long, golden curved horns which hold a moon-like sphere suspended between them. The base version of this Mask is dark brown, with a white forehead and a deep orange and yellow gradient on the eye marks and inner ears. It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item, costing {{Cost|2 SH|season=Radiance}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node.<br><br>

===<span id="Ultimate Cape">Radiance Ultimate Cape</span>===
The Radiance Ultimate [[Cape]] is a long, off-white wrap-around neck scarf with golden accents at the hems and a golden star at the front. The scarf's ends feature wavy bands of orange, green, and light blue. It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item, costing {{Cost|1 SH|season=Radiance}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br><br>

==Additional Cosmetics ==
Upon completing Quests of the Season of Radiance, an additional item is available to be collected for {{Heart}} [[Currency#Hearts|Hearts]] through the Radiance Guide's [[#Quest Tree|Quest Tree]]. '''All items remain in the Ultimate Guide's Quest Tree after the Season ends.'''

===Cape===
The [[Cape]] offered by the Radiance Guide has a white interior and two star-shaped layers, available for {{Cost|12 H}}. It is permanently available to all players after completing the season's [[Season_of_Radiance#Quest_#5|final quest]]. 

The top layer is orange with five points, and the bottom layer is yellow with four. The bottom layer's points poke out from between the top layer's. Both have a trim along the edge in the other layer's color.
