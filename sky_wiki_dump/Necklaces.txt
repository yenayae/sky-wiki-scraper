'''Necklaces''' (including '''Pendants''' and '''Neck Accessories''') is the general term used in Sky for all [[Cosmetics]] that are worn on the player's neck or shoulders. Most of the Necklaces are [[Seasonal Events|Season]] Pendants, given automatically to any players who purchase that Season's [[Season Pass]].

Necklaces share the second [[Outfit Shrine]] from the left with [[Masks]], where they are listed in the third tab (the bowtie {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|neckpiece}} icon). One Necklace, one Mask, and one [[Face Accessory]] can be worn simultaneously but all three can be worn individually as well.
