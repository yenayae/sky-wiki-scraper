This year, ''Sky'' turns 5! And in case you haven't heard, TGC is throwing an extra special party for the occasion. ''Sky'''s 5th Anniversary was called '''SkyFest''', beginning '''Friday, July 12, 2024 at 17:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and continuing until '''Friday, July 26, 2024 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''15''' days. It featured the first-ever simultaneous in-game and in-person [[Sky Assembly]] event for everyone to be a part of. For the duration of the event, the Anniversary Guide appeared in [[Home]] and [[Aviary Village]]. In addition, the [[Secret Area]] in the Vault of Knowledge was open to everyone through a friendly NPC at the gate, with an additional entrance available through the [[Aviary Village#Aviary Event Store|Aviary Event Store]].

Meditate at circles in [[Aviary Village]] near the [[Friendship Constellation]] statue or the gates to the levels. That will take you to a special version of [[Aviary Village]] which will be the center of the [[SkyFest]] party. The town is decorated just for the occasion, and periodically you can watch or even be part of a parade of Spirits who have made their mark on the realms over the years. Three familiar portals will take you to these locations made just for SkyFest:
* History of Sky Gallery (Isle of Dawn gate)
* Art of Sky Gallery (Hidden Forest gate)
* SkyFest Cinema (Eye of Eden gate)

===2024 New Cosmetics and Props===

====SkyFest Wireframe Cape====
::'''''"A special commemoration of Sky's fifth Anniversary. Allows revising the festive transformation of Aviary Village, History of Sky gallery, Art of Sky gallery, and Developer Talk stage."'''''
The SkyFest Wireframe Cape is a blue gradient version of the [[Capes#Base Cape|Base Cape]] with a [[wp:Wire-frame_model|wireframe]] pattern along the edges and a lopsided scarf wrapped around the player's neck. The scarf is a matching blue color with a pale trim, and features a constellation design shaped like a "5". This [[Cape]] was introduced in 2024 and was available for {{Cost|19.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.

This item gives the wearer and the friends they hold hands with a special ability to revisit the festive transformation of [[Aviary Village]], including the Ball Game, Oreo, Parade, and Fireworks events, alongside the History of Sky gallery, Art of Sky gallery, and Developer Talk Stage. The Cape provides access to SkyFest event spaces only, so it '''does not include''' access to the [[Secret Area]] or Meditation Area. The option to revisit the SkyFest festivities is enabled in the [[Expression Menu]]; simply open the [[Menus_and_Controls#Emote_Submenu|Emote Submenu]] with the item equipped and select the Cape's icon to be teleported to the SkyFest version of Aviary Village. <small>(Since this is a numbered item for a specific birthday year, it is not scheduled to return in future years.)<ref name="Numbered items">"As you may know from past events, numbered items for specific birthday years are only available for their given year. After all, you can’t turn five twice!" - {{v|0.26.0}} Patch Notes</ref><br>

====SkyFest Star Jar====
The SkyFest Star Jar is a transparent jar shaped like an 8-pointed star with a cork and a gold handle attached to the top. Inside, it contains five small stars. It is a placeable [[Prop]] that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. When placed down, it can be picked up and held by the handle. This Prop was introduced in 2024 for {{Cost|15 T|event=Skyfest}} from the Anniversary Guide in [[Home]] and the [[Secret Area]] for the duration of the event.<br>
