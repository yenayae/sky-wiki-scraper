<section begin="Summary" />Inspired by the Season of Assembly, '''Sky Assemblies''' is an event program designed to provide players with an opportunity to gather with other fans in their local communities, celebrating Sky as a shared interest and building lasting, life-enriching friendships.

At a Sky Assembly event, players will have the opportunity to safely meet other players while participating in themed activities such as participating in in-game challenges, arts and crafts, trivia, and not to mention, of course, playing Sky!<section end="Summary" /> Sky Assemblies will be hosted by approved volunteer hosts at vetted locations. Authorized events will be announced on the Sky website as well as official social media channels. For FAQs, please visit [https://www.thatskygame.com/news/introducing-sky-assemblies the FAQ section in the news "Introducing Sky Assemblies!"].

This page contains all Sky Assemblies that have been announced in chronological order. Most past event photos and video links are from official social media channels. Contributions on event details (especially from past participants) are welcomed.

Apply to host or find out about [https://www.thatskygame.com/sky-assemblies/ upcoming Sky Assemblies on the official Sky website].

==Long Beach, California, USA (July 21, 2022)==
[[File:assemblieskickoffheader_2022.png|thumb|[https://www.thatskygame.com/news/sky-assemblies-kicks-off-in-july Announcement Banner]]]
The Sky Assemblies Kickoff Event took place on Thursday, July 21, 2022 from 09:00 to 14:00 PDT (UTC-7) at [https://roseparkroasters.com/pages/contact Rose Park Roasters' Pine Avenue location] in Long Beach, California. Over 200 people showed up to the event. <!-- Ref: https://discord.com/channels/575762611111592007/575827782144098304/999929994635657287--> Attendees who completed the New Friend Quest could receive from the Community Team a goodie bags, which contained a keychain, thick transparent emote stickers, a pin, and regular stickers. <!-- Ref: https://discord.com/channels/575762611111592007/575827782144098304/999819498980917308--> Some attendees showed their artistic side during the event by wearing costumes, wearing nail arts, and playing Sky and [https://www.instagram.com/reel/CgSURwIFDjW/ real-life instruments].

A special rule was introduced to this event that attendees must first bow, wave, butterfly pose, boogie, or crab walk before talking to another attendee, and the attendee being emoted at have to return the favor with an emote of their own. The exception to this rule is if you sit at a table or bench with another attendee, you can begin chatting right from the get go as you normally would in Sky.

===[https://www.thatskygame.com/news/sky-assemblies-long-beach-guide Activities]===
*'''Check-In''': Upon arrival, please visit a member of the Community Team to collect your name tag, sign an attendee waiver, and learn more about the events of the day and opportunities to have fun. We encourage you to write your Sky Kid name on your badge, but anything you'd like to go by will do.
*'''Sky Music Lessons (10:00)''': Legendary musician Denise will be offering Sky music lessons. If you've always wanted to express yourself musically in Sky but been unsure how to, there's never been a better time to learn from one of the studio's best!
*'''Valley Racing Grand Prix (10:00)''': If you've got the speed, join Robert for a thrilling, high-octane race through the Valley of Triumph. The prize for winning will be all the friends you made (and passed) along the way!
*'''Sky Trivia (11:00)''': Quiz master and Social Media Manager Sam will be challenging your knowledge of all things Sky.
*'''Sky Scavenger Hunt (12:00)''': Do you know where everything in Sky is? Prove it when Amelia hosts our very first in-person Sky scavenger hunt.
*'''Shall we take a selfie? (12:30)''': At 12:30PM we'll be taking a group selfie to share on official Sky social media channels. Silly faces are required.
*'''New Friend Quest! (all event)''': No Sky-themed event is complete without a quest! Your quest at this Sky Assembly is to make a new friend. Once you've done so, bring yourself and them over to a member of the Community Team and introduce yourselves. We don't have any candles to offer, but we do have cool gift bags that all attendees can receive.
