==<span id="Expression">Expression</span>==
This Spirit offers the {{Emote|Radiance Provoking Performer|inline_text}} [[Expression]], which has 4 Levels.

[[File:{{Icon Name||emote}}|x170px|left]]
<gallery heights="125" captionalign="center">
Radiance-Provoking-Performer-Dance-emote-level-1.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 1
Radiance-Provoking-Performer-Dance-emote-level-2.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 2
Radiance-Provoking-Performer-Dance-emote-level-3.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 3
Radiance-Provoking-Performer-Dance-emote-level-4.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 4
Radiance-Provoking-Performer-Dance-emote-level-3-side.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 3 side
</gallery>
*Level 1 will make your avatar raise their hands in front of their chest, palms loosely facing down, and rhythmically circle them counter clockwise while rocking their body along to the synth beat playing during the Expression. This action is continued for as long as you don't move your avatar. Upon canceling the Expression, your avatar will position their arms parallel to each other, palms facing inwards, before slightly shaking their arms in place.
*Level 2 makes the same motion as Level 1, with a glowing blue circle flickering on each wrist as the dance is performed.
*Level 3 will make your avatar shortly start rocking their body before energetically walking in place with rhythmic steps, rocking their body from side to side and pumping their hands back and forth, all in rhythm with a synth beat playing during the Expression. This action is continued for as long as you don't move your avatar. Upon canceling the Expression, your avatar will come to a halt, tilt their body sideways and cross their arms.
*Level 4 makes the same motion as Level 3, with white starbursts appearing at the hands, white trails following the feet and tiny white sparkles bursting at the ground as the feet touch it. Upon canceling the Expression, all effects disappear and two vertical lines of golden particles appear in front of the legs, before they slowly rise up and fade.

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Spirit is a white sleeveless top with ballooning skirt-like pants. The beige pants have two paler stripes on the front of each pleat. Two thick sashes are tied at the belt and drape around each side of the waist. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|24 SC|season=Radiance}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fifth node ({{Cost|66 SC|season=Radiance}} total).<br>

===<span id="Cape">Cape</span>===
The [[Cape]] offered by this Spirit consists of a thin, X-shaped piece of fabric, whose upper parts are draped over the shoulders and cross in front of the chest. The rest of the main piece hangs down the back; between the X's intersection, another stripe of fabric runs down below it. The Cape is decorated with contrasting zig-zag patterns and angular shapes. The uncolored version of this Cape is beige, with darker and lighter shades of tan for the decorative shapes. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's sixth node ({{Cost|98 SC|season=Radiance}} total).<br>
