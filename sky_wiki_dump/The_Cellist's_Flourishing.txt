<section begin="Summary" />'''The Cellist's Flourishing''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Duets]]. The Cellist's Flourishing is found in the [[Concert Hall]] in [[Aviary Village]], within the curtained hallway behind the stage after completing the [[Season of Duets#Quest #1|first Seasonal Quest]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are an {{Spirit Item|The Cellist's Flourishing|Outfit|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|The Cellist's Flourishing|Cape|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|The Cellist's Flourishing|Prop_r|inline_text|label=Small Pink Rug Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|The Cellist's Flourishing|Prop_p|inline_text|label=Cello Wall Poster Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Shop is a strapless white dress with a pink and blue gradient hem, featuring a blue belt with a geometric pattern tied around the waist. When worn, light blue tattoos appear on the player's bare arms and upper back. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|80 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of two [[Props]]: a [[#Small Blue Rug|Small Pink Rug]] and a [[#Cello Wall Poster|Cello Wall Poster]].

====<span id="Rug Prop">Small Pink Rug</span>====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a small and pink rectangular rug with a dark pink border. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a beige bag with a pink drawstring, containing two pieces of pink, rolled up fabric. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|16 SC|season=Duets}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|16 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

====<span id="Poster Prop">Cello Wall Poster</span>====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a blue and purple rectangular poster with a cello design. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|34 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>
