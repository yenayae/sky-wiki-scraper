<section begin="Summary" />The '''Nesting Loft''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Nesting]]. The Nesting Loft was found within the [[Nesting Workshop]] in [[Aviary Village]], marked outside by a sign with Nesting's emblem. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Loft|Cape|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Loft|prop_b|inline_text|link=Nesting Loft#Bed|label=Bed Prop}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Loft|prop_c|inline_text|link=Nesting Loft#Chair|label=Chair Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Loft|prop_p|inline_text|link=Nesting Loft#Paintings|label=Paintings Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===Cape===
The [[Cape]] offered by this Shop is an angular blue cloak that only covers the player's back, with a pale blue trim and orange interior, somewhat reminiscent of a blueprint or pattern. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|60 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of three [[Props]]: a [[#Bed|Bed]], a [[#Chair|Chair]], and [[#Paintings|Paintings]].

====Bed====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a bed made with a pale wood frame, featuring a curved headboard and yellow bedsheets and pillows. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and seats a single player. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|12 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Chair====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a rounded chair made with pale wood and a yellow pillow on its seat. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and seats a single player. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|20 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|32 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Paintings====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a set of three small paintings with pale wood frames that depict a grassy dome, a [[Manta]], and clouds (reminiscent of the scenery in [[Daylight Prairie]]). It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|36 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|96 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>
