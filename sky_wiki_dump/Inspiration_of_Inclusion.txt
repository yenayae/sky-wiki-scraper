<section begin="Summary" />The '''Inspiration of Inclusion''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Moomin]]. The Inspiration of Inclusion is found on a grassy mound to the right in [[Moominvalley Glade]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|Spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are an {{Spirit Item|Inspiration of Inclusion|Outfit|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Inspiration of Inclusion|Neckpiece|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Inspiration of Inclusion|Hairpiece|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Inspiration of Inclusion|Prop_c|inline_text|label=Grandfather Clock Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Inspiration of Inclusion|Prop_p|inline_text|label=Moominhouse Painting Prop}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of two [[Props]]: a [[#Grandfather Clock|Grandfather Clock]] and a [[#Moominhouse Painting|Moominhouse Painting]].

====Grandfather Clock====
The first [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a tall wooden grandfather clock with a pair of four-pointed stars that function as the clock hands<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. When placed, the smaller star on the clock face moves to indicate the minute of the current hour and continually produces a soft ticking sound. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|12 SC|season=Moomin}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

====Moominhouse Painting====
The second [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a square painting depicting the brightly colored Moominhouse sitting on a hill with a thick wooden frame<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|20 SC|season=Moomin}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's third node.
