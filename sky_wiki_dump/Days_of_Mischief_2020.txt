To celebrate Halloween, Days of Mischief began on '''Thursday, October 22 00:00 and concluded on Wednesday, November 4 at 23:59 (all times PST, UTC-8)'''. The [[Secret Area]] was open to everyone through a friendly NPC at the gate. The [[Secret Area]] was packed with spooky decorations and Dark Dragons.

The Sleepy Traveling Merchant offered free Spell versions of all of the event purchasable items as well as previous year items, on the boat available at [[Home]] for the occasion.

====Mischief Witch Hat====
::'''''"Celebrate Days of Mischief with this enchanting witch hat!"'''''
The Mischief Witch Hat is a black, pointed hat with a wide brim and a short, shaggy haircut. This [[Hair]] was introduced in 2020 for {{Cost|9.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event. Note that this item is considered a Hair, not a [[Hair Accessory]], and as such it cannot be worn over other Hairstyles.<br>{{Dye_Details}}

====Hungry Pumpkin Hat====
::'''''"Celebrate Days of Mischief with this hungry pumpkin-shaped hat"'''''
The Hungry Pumpkin Hat is a pack that contains two items: the Hungry Pumpkin Hat and {{Cost|8 C}}. The Hungry Pumpkin Hat is a carved orange pumpkin with yellow eyes and teeth that sits on top of the player's head. This [[Hair]] was originally introduced in 2019 before returning as a pack for {{Cost|11.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event. Note that this item is considered a Hair, not a [[Hair Accessory]], and as such it cannot be worn over other Hairstyles.<br>{{Dye_Details}}
