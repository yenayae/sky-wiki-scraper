'''Hair''' (also called '''Hairstyles''') are [[Cosmetics]] that cover the player's head. It is the single largest category of cosmetics in the game, featuring a wide variety of styles and designs to choose from. The current Hair is selected in the middle [[Outfit Shrine]], where they are listed in the first tab (the Base Hair {{Icon| Icon_hair_default.png | label=}} icon). The second tab in the Hair Closet (the hat {{Icon| Icon hair summer hat headpiece.png | label= }} icon) lists the available [[Hair Accessories]] and the third tab in the Hair Closet (the horns {{Icon| Icon hair dreams hermit horns.png | label= }} icon) lists the available [[Head Accessories]]. Players can wear one Hair, one Hair Accessory, and one Head Accessory simultaneously.

While the vast majority of Hairs represent the player's hair (or lack of hair), some hats are listed within the Hair category and ''not'' the Hair Accessory category, completely replacing the player's previous Hair. This makes the distinction between a Hair and a Hair Accessory somewhat fluid - and allows players to wear two hats at the same time. Note that almost all of the actual hairs in the game are solid white, with only a few featuring streaks or tint of color.

== <span id="Base Hairstyle">Base Hair</span> ==
This is the Hair worn by the player at the start of the game. It is a short, angular bob with a cowlick at the crown and a longer lock of hair on the right side adorned by a silver and blue band.<br>

Two more base game Hairs were added with Patch {{v|0.23.4}} in November 2023 to supplement the basic styles available to new players. The first is a short, simple cut with tight curls. The second features bangs and a pair of low pigtails that drape over the player's back, tied with a silver and blue band.<br>
