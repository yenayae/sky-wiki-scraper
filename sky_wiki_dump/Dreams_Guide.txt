<section begin="Summary" />The '''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Dreams]] who offers quests and [[Dreams Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Dreams Guide is found near the buildings on the right when entering the [[Village of Dreams]] through the gate located at the [[Valley of Triumph#Coliseum|Coliseum]]. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item||necklace_u|link=#Dreams Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item||mask_u|link=#Dreams Ultimate Mask|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item||cape_u|link=#Dreams Ultimate Cape|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<section end="Summary" />

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Dreams Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Dreams Ultimate [[Necklace|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Dreams Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Dreams only.

===Dreams Ultimate Mask===
The Dreams Ultimate [[Mask]] is a black phoenix-inspired mask with red and yellow accents. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Dreams}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

===Dreams Ultimate Cape===
The Dreams Ultimate Cape is a black and red cape with yellow edges, overall resembling the look of a plumage. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to be purchased for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Dreams}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>

==Quest Tree==
The {{PAGENAME}} Quest Tree is available for all players to complete quests from the Season of Dreams. During the Season of Dreams, the hearts awarded for completing each quest were free. Since the end of the Season, players completing the quests for the first time have to pay {{Candle|3}} to obtain these hearts.
