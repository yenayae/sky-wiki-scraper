This page contains the '''[[Friendship Tree (Regular)|Friendship Trees]]''' for all 37 '''[[Regular Spirits]]''' found in the [[Realms]]. The total [[Currency]] cost to complete all the Realm [[Constellations]] can be found in the [[#Sumary|Summary]] section. To complete the entire Constellation, players must purchase all [[Cosmetics]] from all Regular Spirits. Bonus items that do not count towards the completion of the Realm Constellations are identified with a note. 

==Summary==
These are the total number of {{Candle}}Candles, {{Heart}} Hearts, and {{Ascended Candle}}Ascended Candles required to complete the Realm Constellations. Note that Tier 2 is '''not''' required for completion.
