<section begin="Summary" />The Divining Wise Grandparent is a [[Seasonal Spirit]] from the [[Season of the Blue Bird]]. The Divining Wise Grandparent is found at the Blue Bird Theater in the Elevated Clearing from [[Hidden Forest]]. The collectibles [[#Traveling Spirit|offered]] by this Spirit are a {{Spirit Item|Divining Wise Grandparent|Facepiece|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Divining Wise Grandparent|Cape|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Face Accessory">Face Accessory</span>===
The [[Face Accessory]] offered by this Spirit is a headpiece with silver curls sprouting from a diamond-shaped base. The central vane has glowing symbols at the top. It was available to all players for {{Cost|14 SC|season=Blue Bird}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|21 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br><br>

===<span id="Cape">Cape</span>===
The [[Cape]] offered by this Spirit is butterfly-shaped, with a starry interior. The high-collared shoulder mantle features two silver moons and tassels. The Cape is decorated with a scalloped silver line, and trimmed in the same color. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|59 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br><br>
