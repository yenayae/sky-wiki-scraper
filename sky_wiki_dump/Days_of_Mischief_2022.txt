The Days of Mischief celebration return for a fourth edition, and the [[Secret Area]] awaited all of you for the party! The event began '''Monday, October 24, 2022 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and ended '''Sunday, November 13, 2022 at 23:59 PST (UTC-8)''', lasting '''21''' days.

For the occasion, the [[The Cackling Crab|Cackling Crab]] is once more available with just as much mischief as in previous years. More new fun features are added: a rideable [[Krill|Dark Dragon]] and a "not so friendly cat" that will turn you into a cursed figure of mayhem. ''But be aware that this Spell has special power!''

[[Spells]] and items from all previous Days of Mischief are also available to choose from by speaking to the Mischief Spirits in [[The Cackling Crab]] and at the Sleepy Traveling Merchant's boat. The boat had additional Crab Rock Trick, Candle Trick, Dark Dragon Repellent, and Witch Hat Accessory Spells as well.

<small>Note: Days of Mischief content is available to all players who have progressed to at least Vault of Knowledge.</small>

===2022 New Cosmetics and Props===
====Feline Familiar Prop====
::'''''"Can you pet the cat? Only at your own risk."'''''
The Feline Familiar Prop is a fluffy black cat that appears inanimate. It is a placeable [[Prop]] that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. Once placed, any players that attempt to pet the Feline Familiar will be launched backwards as its fur puffs up and it yowls with displeasure. This Prop was introduced in 2022 for {{Cost|9.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.<br>

====Cat Costume Pack<span id="Cat Mask"></span><span id="Cat Cape"></span>====
::'''''"A feline-inspired Cape and Mask for when you're feeling furry."'''''
The Cat Costume Pack contains two cosmetics: the Cat Cape and Cat Mask. The Cat Cape is a black [[Cape]] that resembles a cat's tail attached to a furry collar. The Cat Mask is a black [[Mask]] with a cat-like snout, angry eyes, and a red forehead decoration. This pack was introduced in 2022 for {{Cost|19.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.<br>

===2022 New Spells===
====Candle Trick Spell====
The Candle Trick Spell temporarily places a red Candle on the player's head for 8 minutes. Play tricks on other players by burrowing and pretending to be a Candle. Players hidden in the ground can jump back out to spook anyone who tries to light the Candle. This [[Spell]] was introduced in 2022 for free from the Gift Crab in the Cackling Crab's main area and from the Sleepy Traveler's boat at Home for {{Cost|5 C}}.<br>

==== Krill Cat's Curse Spell ====
The Krill Cat's Curse Spell temporarily transform players into mischievous dusty figures with spikey hair for 30 minutes. Approaching any players and using the {{Emote|Scarecrow Farmer|inline_text}} [[Expression]] will spread the curse and turn them into dusty figures as well. This Spell was introduced in 2022 and is not collectible; it was only available with the Krill Cat wandering around [[The Cackling Crab]]. When it later returned in 2023, the Spell was only available with the NPC Spirit standing at The Cackling Crab's entrance '''on the first week of the event'''.<br>
