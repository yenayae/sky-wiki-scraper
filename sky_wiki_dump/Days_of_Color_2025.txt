Days of Color returned for a sixth year! The event began on '''Monday, May 26, 2025 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and continued until '''Sunday, June 8, 2025 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''14''' days. Rainbows brightened the skies above the [[Daylight Prairie#Prairie Village|Prairie Village]], with new games, a colorful race, and more! For the duration of the event, the Color Guide stood at [[Home]] and [[Aviary Village]], offering to teleport players to the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area|Daylight Prairie's Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. 

Most event activities from previous years returned unchanged, with the exception being the Rainbow Race from [[Days of Color/2024|2024]]. The race was updated to have Colored Butterflies containing random [[Dyes#Collecting Colored Light and Dyes|Colored Light]] flying around each checkpoint, and a photo shoot spot at the end of the race.   

The [[Radiance Guide]] also arrived to host three new mini-games; The Crab Toss game, the Concentration Game, and the Shell Game. Winning a mini-game rewarded players with random Colored Light, and Event Tickets Light equal to 2.5 Event Tickets.  
*The Crab Toss Game: To win, toss three crabs into the vat 
*The Concentration Game: To win, reveal symbols 2 at a time until you find all matching pairs
*The Shell Game: To win, spot the chest with the colored light

Sky continued its partnership to support the Trevor Project, a leading suicide prevention and mental health organization for LGBTQ youth, providing crisis services, education, and peer support to help young people thrive. Between five (({{Cost|5.00 USD|refnote}})) and ten dollars ({{Cost|10.00 USD|refnote}}) were donated on behalf of each new and returning [[Premium Candle Shop|IAP]] purchases from this event.

===Ticket Locations===
[[File:Days-of-Color-Rainbow-Ticket.png|125px|thumb|A Days of Color rainbow Ticket]]
A total of {{Cost|5 T|event=color}} Event Tickets could be forged by participating in two of these event activities each day:
*The Crab Toss Game
*The Concentration Game
*The Shell Game
*The Rainbow Race
In addition, {{Cost|15 T|event=nature}} one-time Event Tickets shaped like Rainbows were hidden throughout the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. They could be collected at any time during the event. 

Players could also buy additional Tickets (costing {{Cost|2 AC}} or {{Cost|6 C}} each) from the Color Gift Box found at the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]].

===2025 New Cosmetics and Props===
====Rainbow Face Paint Mask====
::'''''"Throw some color on your face, and in your path leave a colorful trace. We pledge to donate $10.00 (USD) of the purchase price from this item to charities championing diversity and global unity"'''''
The Rainbow Facepaint contains three items: the Rainbow Facepaint Mask, {{Cost|75 C}}, and {{Cost|D|color=white}}x 3. The [[Mask]] is a bare face with paint in a domino mask-like shape, and a four-pointed star on the forehead. The paint is a gradient from orange to yellow. This pack was introduced in 2025 and was available for {{Cost|19.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.<br>

====Rainbow Ribbon Shawl====
::'''''"The vibrant ribbons and radiant interior of this shawl beautifully showcase the dyed colors."'''''
The Rainbow Ribbon Shawl is a pleated cape with a tri-colored drape of fabric around it. The [[Cape]] is a gradient from white to black, with golden toothed outlines and white interior. The colored fabric is fastened at the neck with golden diamonds. This Cape was introduced in 2024 and was available for {{Cost|14.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.<br>

====Rainbow Smock====
The Rainbow Smock is an oversized shirt with a long apron, rolled up-pants, and boots. The shirt features a turtleneck and rolled-up sleeves. The apron is a long strip with a hole for the head, draped over the body. It features a pale gold toothed outline and rim, three diamond shapes down the center of the chest, and is tied in the back. The boots have a stripe down the front. The [[Outfit]] has splotches of bright color on the bottoms of the sleeves and the shoes.

This Outfit was introduced in 2025 and was available for {{Cost|42 T|event=color}} from the Color Guide at the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]] for the duration of the event.<br>

====Rainbow Head Wrap====
The Rainbow Head Wrap is a [[Hair]] with a beanie-like hat. The white hat has golden toothed outlines, and three orange-yellow bands of fabric wrapped around it. One side of the hair is braided and rests on the shoulder, and the other cropped to ear-length.
This Hair was introduced in 2025 and was available for {{Cost|20 T|event=color}} from the Color Guide at the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]] for the duration of the event.<br>
