'''Days of Giving''' is an annual event celebrating the generosity and gratitude of Thanksgiving that happens around the end of November. As it's primarily a [[Double Currency Events|Double Currency event]], there are no cosmetics offered. 

==December 2024<span id="2024"></span>==
<section begin="2024" />
[[File:Day-of-giving-december2024-sns.png|left|thumb|180px|Days of Giving - December 2024]]
[[Days of Giving]] returned for two weeks to double the Hearts and Gifts of Light players sent to each other. The event approximately began on '''Monday, December 9, 2024 at 00:00 PST (UTC-8)''' and continued until '''Sunday, December 22, 2024 at 23:59 PST (UTC-8)'''. All Hearts and Light Gifts sent while the event was active were doubled for the recipient.

The event also included a doubling of Seasonal Light and Treasure Candles. Players could find eight Seasonal Candle Bunches instead of four in the selected Realm, and eight Daily Treasure Candles instead of four in the selected Realm marked by the two lit Red Candles.<section end="2024" />
{{Clear}}

==November 2022<span id="2022"></span>==
<section begin="2022" />[[File:Day-of-giving-november2022.png|left|thumb|180px|Days of Giving - November 2022]]
[[Days of Giving]] returned for one week to double the Hearts and Gifts of Light players sent to each other. The event approximately began '''Tuesday, November 22, 2022 at 00:00 PST (UTC-8)''' and continued until '''Monday, November 28, 2022 at 23:59 PST (UTC-8)'''. All Hearts and Light Gifts sent while the event was active were doubled for the recipient.

Unexpectedly, the event also included a doubling of Treasure Candles. Players could find eight Daily Treasure Candles instead of four in the selected Realm marked by the two lit Red Candles. No official announcement was made concerning this Double Light event, though it could easily be observed in-game.<section end="2022" />

The event approximately began '''Tuesday, November 26, 2019 at 00:00 PST (UTC-8)''' and continued until '''Tuesday, December 4, 2019 at 12:00 PST (UTC-8)'''<ref>Originally scheduled to end on Tuesday, December 3, 2019 at 12:00 PST, Days of Giving was extended to another day as a thank you for players' support in being chosen as iPhone Game of the Year.</ref>.
