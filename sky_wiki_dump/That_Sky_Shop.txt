<section begin="Summary" />'''That Sky Shop''' (stylized as [https://www.thatskyshop.com/ thatskyshop]) is the only official merchandise shop of ''Sky: Children of Light.'' First opened on July 17, 2020, the website sells a variety of pins, gift boxes, clothing, plushes, and more. The merchandise comes in waves with most of the items changing in between; TGC has said that some items [https://www.thatskyshop.com/pages/faqs may return to the catalogue]. Almost all items include a thematic poem and all pins have [https://www.thatskyshop.com/pages/whatisstar STAR features].<section end="Summary" />

Additional official Sky merchandise (not sold on That Sky Shop) can be found on the [[Sky Merch Collaborations]] page.

==STAR Features==
Visit the [[STAR]] page to get more information on Compatibility, Troubleshooting and STAR feature durations and cooldown. 

[https://www.thatskyshop.com/blogs/news/welcome-to-the-star-family STAR Protectors] (NFC chips) are embedded in plushes and tags of all Sky Apparel. Scanning the STAR Protector in-game reveals an authenticity message.

==Evergreen Items==
Currently, there are two items that are "Evergreen", which means they should be available during every wave: TGC pin and [[That Sky Shop/Wave 2#Lightseeker Umbrella|Lightseeker Umbrella]] (introduced in Wave 2).

''Pick up at Shinjuku Sumitomo Sankaku Hiroba''<br>

* Official Sky merch including a tote bag, 5th Anniversary T-shirt, an official [[That Sky Shop/Wave 11# The Art of Sky|The Art of Sky artbook]], and an autographed thank-you card
* Artbook signing priority voucher
* Main Stage VIP Access
* A special extra [[Quests|Daily Quest]] reward (if you also complete the quest, you will get two rewards in total)
* Meet and greet session with Jenova

Session 1: 9:00-13:00<br>
Session 2: 14:00-18:00<br>
