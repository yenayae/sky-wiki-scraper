'''Player Creations''' refer to any player-made content in-game such as [[Message Candle Spots|Message Candles]], [[Blessings (Spells)#Message_Boat_Spell|Message Boats]], [[Shared Memories Candles|Shared Memories]], or [[Shared Spaces]]. Each Player Creation requires at least one 'like' from a friend or follower before the creation becomes visible to other players. Players who view a Player Creation can send the creator a [[Currency#Hearts|Heart]] {{Cost|H}} for three candles {{Cost|C}} once they've liked the creation. You don't have to be friends with a player to send them a heart, and you can send them a heart through the creation in addition to gifting them a regular heart every day.

==Trivia==
As of Patch {{v|0.25.0}}, players can no longer 'passively' receive a heart {{Cost|H}} from earning 'likes'. Previously, players could earn one heart after receiving enough likes on a Player Creation. Each type of creation could only earn one heart per cooldown. For example, a Player could only earn one heart from any Shared Space in 4 days.
