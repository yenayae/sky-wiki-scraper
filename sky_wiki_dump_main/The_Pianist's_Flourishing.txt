<section begin="Summary" />'''The Pianist's Flourishing''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Duets]]. The Pianist's Flourishing is found in the [[Concert Hall]] in [[Aviary Village]], within the curtained hallway behind the stage after completing the [[Season of Duets#Quest #1|first Seasonal Quest]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are an {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Flourishing|Outfit|inline_text}}, {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Flourishing|Shoes|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Flourishing|Prop|inline_text|label=Piano Wall Poster Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Shop is a sleeveless dark blue tailcoat with gold hems and a pair of wide, dark blue pants with a light blue geometric pattern wrapping around the hem. When worn, light blue tattoos appear on the player's bare arms. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|60 SC|season=Duets}} total)<br>

===<span id="Shoes">Shoes</span>===
The [[Shoes]] offered by this Shop is a pair of light gray boots with both the heels and toes exposed. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|22 SC|season=Duets}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|34 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Prop</span>===
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a light and dark blue rectangular poster with a piano keyboard design. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|12 SC|season=Duets}} total)<br>
