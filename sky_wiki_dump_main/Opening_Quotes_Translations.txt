This page is a master list of all 602 Opening Quotes for all the languages currently supported by Sky. They are listed here exactly as they appear in the game, including punctuation and capitalization, as well as any typos and errors that are present in the game<!-- If you see a typo or error in any translations of the Opening Quotes, please DO NOT correct it. Check if it has been fixed in the ACTUAL GAME before editing this page. This page is meant to display all Opening Quotes exactly as shown in the game — without modifications or corrections. -->. New areas may be added and existing Opening Quotes may change at any time. For the list of only English Opening Quotes, see the main page [[Opening Quotes]].

For languages with non-Latin based scripts (Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Thai), the romanization of the names and quotes are included in square brackets (“<code>[abc]</code>”). 

Notes on romanizations:

* For Chinese, the romanisations for Chinese (Simplified) are based on the Mandarin dialect and the Hanyu Pinyin romanization scheme, whereas romanizations for Chinese (Traditional) are based on the Cantonese dialect and the Jyutping (LSHK) scheme.

* For Japanese, all-Kana script (Hiragana and Katakana) is also included in parentheses  (“<code>(かな)</code>”).

* For Korean and Vietnamese, the traditional ideographic scripts of these languages (Hanja/漢字 for Korean, Chữ Nôm/𡨸喃 for Vietnamese) are included in parentheses  (“<code>(字)</code>”).

* The romanizations and Hanja/Chữ Nôm on this page are NOT part of the official content of the game. It is provided here for your reference only as aids for pronunciation, understanding, and language learning.

=== Valley of Triumph ===
The Valley of Triumph has four Seasonal Areas with an Opening Quote: Village of Dreams and Hermit Valley (both introduced in the [[Season of Dreams]], 2021-01-04 to 2021-03-15), and Village Theater and Harmony Hall (both introduced in the [[Season of Performance]], 2022-04-11 to 2022-06-26).

Note: In the localized versions of the Opening Quotes for Hermit Valley, a number of them are not a direct translation of the English version but a completely different version roughly meaning "A perilous place for the audacious artists." These versions are marked with a <ref name="not a direct translation">Not a direct translation of the English version but a completely different version that roughly means "A perilous place for the audacious artists."</ref>.<br>

=== Vault of Knowledge ===
The Vault of Knowledge has four areas with an Opening Quote: Starlight Desert, Dream of A Little Friend, and Land of Tears (all added in the [[Season of The Little Prince]], 2021-07-06 to 2021-09-19), and Repository of Refuge ([[Season of Remembrance]], 2023-01-16 to 2023-04-02). 

The Sky Memorial was added to the Starlight Desert in June 2023 after the passing of Don Heim, TGC's IT Security Manager.

The Dream of A Little Friend seasonal area changed its name to Belt of Asteroids some time between 0.24.2 to 0.29.2.<br>

== Location of the Opening Quotes ==
* '''Isle of Dawn:''' The Opening Quote is displayed after passing the first ledge in the Sand Dunes area, after passing the first [[Map Shrine]] and [[Child of Light]].
* '''Daylight Prairie''': The Opening Quote is displayed '''only once''', before the flight into the Villages. 
* '''Hidden Forest''': The Opening Quote is displayed in the Forest Clearing, when opening the second gate.
* '''Valley of Triumph''': The Opening Quote is displayed after the cutscenes of the opening of the gates just before the start of the Sliding Race or the Flying Race. 
* '''Golden Wasteland''': The Opening Quote is displayed during the black cutscenes before landing flat on the ground in the Broken Temple area.
* '''Vault of Knowledge''': The Opening Quote is displayed after leaving the Social Space and upon entering the First Floor of the Vault.
* '''Eye of Eden:''' The first Opening Quote ("Push into the darkness") is displayed after passing through the first section (Wind and Rocks), when the gate to the second section ([[Dark Dragons]]) opens.
* '''Eye of Eden''' (Second Part/After Storm Lock): The second Opening Quote ("Return the light of the fallen") is displayed immediately after the cutscenes of entry into the Statues section.

== Trivia ==
* The Opening Quote for Daylight Prairie is displayed only once for each Sky account, meaning that to view this Opening Quote again, you will need to create a new Sky account and play from the beginning. 
* The Valley of Triumph opening quote appears relatively late in the Realm, allowing players to explore the Sliding Track (after the Social Space), the Ice Rink, and the Citadel, before seeing the Opening Quote. 
* Players can take damage (and potentially lose [[Winged Light]]) before seeing the Opening Quotes in Hidden Forest (from the rain) and Eye of Eden (from the flying rocks).
* Localizations:
** Eye of Eden has different names/titles between the first and second Opening in Japanese. The first title is "暴風域" which literally means "Storm Zone," and the second title (after passing the Point of No Return) is "原罪," which means "Original Sin." 
*** Similar differences also happen in Mainland China, on the Simplified Chinese version of Sky operated by NetEase. The first title is the same as the international version ("暴风眼," Eye of the Storm), but the second title is "伊甸之眼," which is a literal translation from the English "Eye of Eden."
** In four localizations, the translations of "Eye of Eden" do not include a direct or phonetic transliteration of the word "Eden."
*** The Simplified and Traditional Chinese versions have "暴风眼/暴風眼," which means "Eye of the Storm."
*** The Japanese version, as mentioned above, have "Storm Zone" and "Original Sin," with the latter mentioned another Biblical term.
*** The Vietnamese version has "Mắt Địa Đàng," which means "Eye of the Heaven."
** The Chinese and Japanese versions of Wasteland's Opening Quote do not mention the Vault of Knowledge, as did the original English version.
<!--* Even though the Opening Quotes for the Home and Aviary are no longer displayed, they are still included in the <code>Localizable.strings</code> file in the macOS version package and translated into the newly added languages (Vietnamese and Indonesian).-->
* Some sub-areas within a Realm do not have an Opening Quote, such as the Sand Dune Cave in Isle of Dawn, the Cave in Prairie, the Underground Cavern in Forest, the Archives and Office in Vault of Knowledge, and the Orbit after Eye of Eden.
* Some localization of the Opening Quote of Hermit Valley is a completely different sentence than the English version ("A perilous place for the audacious artists"). This may be a leftover from previous versions of this opening.
