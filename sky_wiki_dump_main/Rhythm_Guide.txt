<section begin="Summary" />The '''Rhythm Guide''' is a Seasonal Guide from the [[Season of Rhythm]] who offered [[Rhythm Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]] and no Quests. The Rhythm Guide was only found at [[Home]] during the Season. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item||necklace_u|link=Rhythm Guide#Rhythm Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item||mask_u|link=Rhythm Guide#Rhythm Ultimate Mask|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item||hair_u|link=Rhythm Guide#Rhythm Ultimate Hair|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<section end="Summary" />

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Rhythm Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Rhythm Seasonal [[Neck Accessory|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Rhythm Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Rhythm only.

===<span id="Ultimate Mask">Rhythm Ultimate Mask</span>===
The Rhythm Ultimate [[Mask]] is round and dark gray, with a metallic, golden triangle placed under each eye. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Rhythm}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Hair">Rhythm Ultimate Hair</span>===
(''Not to be confused with the [[Performance Guide]]'s Hair.'')<br>
<br>
The Rhythm Ultimate [[Hair]] is a ponytail with chin-length sections of hair framing each side of the face, triangle-shaped bangs, and two feather-like strands on top that stick up and back. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|4 SH|season=Rhythm}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>

==Quest Tree==
Unlike later Ultimate Guides, the Rhythm Guide did not have a Quest Tree since there were no Seasonal Quests. Instead, the [[Season of Rhythm#Spirits and Cosmetics|Seasonal Spirits]] were individually released over the course of the Season, with one added each week.
