<section begin="Summary" />'''The Pianist's Beginnings''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Duets]]. The Pianist's Beginnings is found in the [[Concert Hall]] in [[Aviary Village]], within the curtained hallway behind the stage after completing the [[Season of Duets#Quest #1|first Seasonal Quest]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are an {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Beginnings|Outfit|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Beginnings|Hair|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Beginnings|Prop_r|inline_text|label=Small Blue Rug Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|The Pianist's Beginnings|Prop_p|inline_text|label=Piano Keys Wall Poster Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Shop is dark gray with a sleeveless top and baggy pants with a blue geometric pattern wrapping around the knees, paired with a light gray waist wrap secured by a maroon belt. When worn, light yellow tattoos appear on the player's bare wrists. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|58 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

===<span id="Hair">Hair</span>===
The [[Hair]] offered by this Shop is a short and spikey slicked-back half ponytail, tied with a blue hairband. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|20 SC|season=Duets}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|34 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of two [[Props]]: a [[#Small Blue Rug|Small Blue Rug]] and a [[#Piano Keys Wall Poster|Piano Keys Wall Poster]].

====<span id="Rug Prop">Small Blue Rug</span>====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a small and blue rectangular rug with a yellow border. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a beige bag with a blue drawstring, containing two blue, rolled up pieces of fabric. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|30 SC|season=Duets}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|88 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>

====<span id="Poster Prop">Piano Keys Wall Poster</span>====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a long, blue and purple rectangular poster with a piano keys design. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|14 SC|season=Duets}} total).<br>
