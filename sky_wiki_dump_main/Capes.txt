== Cape Energy ==
[[File:Cape_Star_Pattern.png|frameless|400px|thumb|center|Cape Star Pattern]]
The back of the player's Cape has a line of symbols showing their current Cape energy. In addition, any time the player is recharging, the entire Cape undergoes a color-change animation. This allows players to see each other energy levels, for example to know when a friend needs to be recharged.  The Cape symbols are not, however, a direct indication of a player's experience because there are ways to lose Cape wedges.

On most Capes, the symbols are stars/diamonds, but specialty Capes may have season-specific or event-specific symbols.  The number of symbols (including the outlines/frames) matches the player's total number of wedges.  In other words, the 6th-10th Cape wedges are each represented by an outline around the 1st-5th diamond.  The 11th and 12th wedges (the current maximum) are a second outline around the top two symbols. These symbols glow white if the corresponding Cape wedge is charged.  They are dark if you have used up your energy.

== Tier 2 Capes ==
All [[Regular Spirit]] Capes have two levels (Tiers) of Cape. The Tier 2 Capes are the same overall color as the corresponding Tier 1 versions, but have a secondary accent color. They always cost three times as much as the Tier 1 Capes.
<gallery widths="125px">
CourageousSoldier-3-Morybel-0146.png|Green Tier 1 Cape icon
Cape green2.png|Green Tier 1 Cape
CourageousSoldier-4.png|Green Tier 2 Cape icon
Cape green tier 2 back.png|Green Tier 2 Cape
</gallery>

The Tier 2 Capes are intended to be bonus content for long-time players.  They are always unlocked by a second [[Wing Buff]] that requires a large number of [[Ascended Candle]]s.  Neither the second Wing Buff nor the Tier 2 Cape need to be purchased as part of completing a Realm's [[Constellation]].

== Special Capes ==
In general, the only effect of changing your Cape is changing the visual appearance of your Cape. However, there are some Capes with special functions.
* The [[Secret Area#Beta Cape|Beta Cape]] and [[Secret Area#Founder's Pack|Founder's Cape]] gives players access to the [[Secret Area]]. 
* The [[Sky for Nintendo Switch#Nintendo Pack|Nintendo Blue and Red Capes]] gives players access to the [[Golden Wasteland#Nintendo Switch Area|Nintendo Area]].
* The [[Season of The Little Prince#Little Prince Asteroid Jacket|Little Prince Asteroid Jacket]] gives players access to the [[Season of The Little Prince#Dreams of A Little Friend|Dreams of A Little Friend]] area.
* The [[AURORA Concert#Wings of AURORA|Wings of AURORA]] gives players access to the [[AURORA Concert|Aurora Concert]].
* The [[Hopeful Steward#Revival Ultimate Cape|Revival Ultimate Cape]] creates a rainbow light effect when flying through the air.
* The [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer#Radiance of the Nine-Colored Deer|Radiance of the Nine-Colored Deer]] gives the wearer and the friends they hold hands with a special ability to directly transport to a tranquil location and enjoy the company of that Season’s legendary creature when it periodically enters the space.
* The [[Sky Anniversary/2024#SkyFest Wireframe Cape|SkyFest Wireframe Cape]] and [[Sky Anniversary#TGC Wireframe Cape|TGC Wireframe Cape]] gives the wearer and the friends who holding hands with them the ability to go to the [[Sky Anniversary]] version of [[Aviary Village]] and play the activities, and revisit the art and history of Sky museums.
* The [[Season_of_Moomin#Moominmamma's_Masterpiece|Moominmamma's Masterpiece Cape]] gives the wearer, and the friends who holding hands with them, the ability to go inside the Moominhouse. 
* The [[Blue Bird Guide#Blue Bird Ultimate Cape|Blue Bird Ultimate Cape]] creates a blue light effect when flying, and produces the illusion of blue feathers falling from the cape every time the player spends light to fly. 
* The [[Vault Elder's Lantern#Two Embers Part 1 Ultimate Cape|Two Embers Part 1 Ultimate Cape]] offers the wearer and friends they hold hand with the special ability to fly in the seasonal area, as well an entrance into a safe, peaceful place within the area.

== Trivia==
[[File:Deer-Cape-Stars-Colors.png|thumb|200px|The Radiance of the Nine-Colored Deer Cape stars with colors (top to bottom): blue, yellow, orange, red, and purple.]]
*In [[Sky: Light Awaits]], the Capes were much more detailed. However, they were simplified due to performance issues.<ref>[https://discord.com/channels/575762611111592007/583370574449999880/786762853113397279 Posting by Ktjn] on the official Discord server.</ref>
*The Base Cape is featured in a piece of merch from thatskyshop, in the form of a [[That Sky Shop/Wave 4#Cape Mug (Partially Cancelled)|Cape Mug]].
*The Starter Pack Cape is featured in a piece of merch from thatskyshop, in the form of a [[That Sky Shop/Wave 6#thatskykid|thatskykid]]. It has a STAR feature which allows you to meet thatskykid for a limited duration.
*Unlike other capes, each of the Radiance of the Nine-Colored Deer Cape stars has a unique color.
