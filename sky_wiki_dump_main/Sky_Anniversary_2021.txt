===2021 New Cosmetics and Props===

====2nd Birthday Hat====
The 2nd Birthday Hat is a light blue beret with two ribbon tails. This [[Hair Accessory]] was introduced in 2021 for {{Cost|3 H}} from the Anniversary Guide. It was only available during Sky's 2021 Anniversary.

==== Anniversary Birthday Flag====
The Anniversary Birthday Flag (also known as  Sky Anniversary Pennants) is a set of colorful flags strung between two poles. It is a placeable [[Prop]] that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality.  This Prop was introduced in 2021 for {{Cost|20 C}} from the Anniversary Guide. It appears as a smaller version of the flags placed down on its owner's back when carried.
