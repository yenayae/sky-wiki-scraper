'''Outfits''' (including '''Pants''' and '''Leggings''') are [[Cosmetics]] that cover the player's body under their [[Cape]]. In situations where the player does not have a Cape (e.g., in the [[Season of Prophecy#The Trials|Trials]]), the entire Outfit is visible. The current Outfit is selected in the left-most [[Outfit Shrine]], where they are listed in the first and third tab (the The Moomin Storybook Outfit {{Icon|Moomin-Invisible-Outfit-icon.png}} and Talented Builder Outfit {{Icon|Icon season of flight pants 2 talented builder.png}} icons).

Originally, only the player's legs were affected by the choice of Outfit, which is why most of the [[Regular Spirits]]' offerings are depicted as only pants or leggings.  However, since then multiple [[Seasonal Spirits]] and [[Special Events]] have introduced Outfits that also alter the player's top.
{{clear}}
== Base Outfit ==
This is the default Outfit worn by the player at the start of the game. It is a beige half-sleeve top with a pair of matching shorts.<br>
