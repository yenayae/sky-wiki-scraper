<section begin="Summary" />The Radiance Leaping Dancer is a [[Seasonal Spirit]] from the [[Season of Radiance]]  who offers the {{Emote|Radiance Leaping Dancer|inline_text}} Expression. The Radiance Leaping Dancer was found in the [[Aviary Village]], to the left of the Village Plaza's [[Constellation Table]]. The collectibles [[#Traveling Spirit|offered]] by this Spirit are an {{Spirit Item|Radiance Leaping Dancer|Outfit|inline_text}}, {{Spirit Item|Radiance Leaping Dancer|Shoes|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Leaping Dancer|Hair|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Leaping Dancer|Cape|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Radiance Leaping Dancer|Prop|label=Curtain Wall Prop|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==<span id="Expression">Expression</span>==
This Spirit offers the {{Emote|Radiance Leaping Dancer|inline_text}} [[Expression]], which has 4 Levels.

[[File:{{Icon Name||emote}}|x170px|left]]
<gallery heights="125" captionalign="center">
Radiance-Leaping-Dancer-cartwheel-emote-level-1.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 1
Radiance-Leaping-Dancer-cartwheel-emote-level-2.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 2
Radiance-Leaping-Dancer-cartwheel-emote-level-3.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 3
Radiance-Leaping-Dancer-cartwheel-emote-level-4.gif|{{Icon Name||emote_name}} Level 4
</gallery>
*Level 1 will make your avatar crouch slightly, shift one leg before the other and slightly lift their hands at their sides, while a tinkling sound effect plays. This Expression will temporarily replace your basic walk and can be cancelled by flying up once. When moved, your avatar will raise their arms and leap sideways towards the ground to gain momentum and perform one cartwheel after another. A playful descending tone plays during each cartwheel.
*Level 2 makes the same motion as Level 1, with white dust clouds appearing where the feet hit the ground after each completed cartwheel.
*Level 3 will make your avatar lift their arms straight into the air and put one pointed foot forward, while several tinkling sound effects play. This Expression will temporarily replace your basic walk and can be cancelled by flying up once. When moved, your avatar will leap forward to stand on their hands, gain momentum and perform one cartwheel after another. The same playful descending tone as in Level 1 plays during each cartwheel.
*Level 4 makes the same motion as Level 3, with colorful trails of light following the motion of the feet and hands, resulting in a colorful trail of big circles. The right foot and hand have a yellow trail which fades to green, the left hand has a white trail which is only turquoise at the base and turns completely pink, and the left foot has a fully red trail.

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Spirit is an off-white tunic, with draping beige sleeves and pants underneath. The pants cinch just above the ankle. A sash with golden diamond-shaped ornaments is looped loosely from the tunic's waistbelt, and features similar pale accents to the sleeves. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fifth node ({{Cost|72 SC|season=Radiance}} total).<br>

===<span id="Cape">Cape</span>===
The [[Cape]] offered by this Spirit is a beige, gold-rimmed mantle that splits into three kite-shaped tails, both with pale diamond-shaped patterns. Two floating ribbons attach at the mantle and loop around the arms. The ribbons and cape sections are tipped with a gold diamond ornament each. The uncolored version of this Cape is beige, with a paler cream for the two outer tails, and off-white for the ribbons. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|28 SC|season=Radiance}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's sixth node ({{Cost|100 SC|season=Radiance}} total).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Prop</span>===
The [[Prop]] offered by this Spirit is a large red curtain attached with red string between two wooden poles, and with a yellow triangular ribbon, decorated with white ornaments, hanging on both sides. When carried on a player's back, the Prop appears as two poles wrapped with the red curtain and tied together with the ribbon. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|28 SC|season=Radiance}} total).<br>
