<section begin="Summary" />The '''Sense of Self''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Moomin]]. The Sense of Self is found on a grassy mound to the right in [[Moominvalley Glade]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|Spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Shoes|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Neckpiece|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Sense of Self|Hairpiece|inline_text}}, and {{Music Sheet|42|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Shoes">Shoes</span>===
The [[Shoes]] offered by this Shop are orange and covered by white leg warmers decorated with a small diamond near the hem<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|30 SC|season=Moomin}} total).<br>

===<span id="Necklace">Neck Accessory</span>===
The [[Neck Accessory]] offered by this Shop is a small silver bell attached to an orange ribbon that wraps around the player's neck<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. When equipped, this item adds a gentle bell jingling sound effect to the player's movements. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|24 SC|season=Moomin}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's third node. For information about the [[Bell Call]], please visit [[Bell Call|here]].<br>
