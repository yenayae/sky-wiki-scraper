This page lists all '''Spirit Visits''' of [[Traveling Spirits]] and [[Returning Spirits]] from Special Visits, as well as statistics based on these visits. See individual pages for information about each type of visit.

==Historical Appearances==
===Appearances by Spirit===
[[File:Traveling_Spirit_playlist.PNG|thumb|303x303px|Video Guide Playlist (up to Season of Assembly) â€“ videos by Tara's Sky Journey (@Ilivetowrite)|link=https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKh97ggSLvdy2pb2aa5HIA5u9k6lEqilq]]The following table summarizes all of the Spirit visits to date, with the most recent Spirits at the top. Spirits who have never returned are included at the end of the table. Details about each Spirit are provided on the linked article. See [[Timeline]] page for full chronological history.

Columns include:
*'''Visits''': Total number of official visits for the Spirit (error appearances are not included in the number of visits)
*'''Visit#''': Traveling Spirit (TS) or the Returning Spirits' Special Visit (SV) Number. All visits for the Spirit are listed, one per line. Error appearances are also listed, but with "Error" for the # (see Spirit page for details of Error visits). Clicking on the Visit# header sorts the row according to each Spirit's '''first visit''' (whereas the Date header sorts according to each Spirit's last visit)
*'''Date''': The start date for each visit. All visits for the Spirit are listed, one per line. For Spirits who have never been a Traveling Spirit, the date of the Season end is provided instead in italics. Clicking on the Date header sorts the row according to each Spirit's '''last visit''' which means that the top of the table lists the Spirits who have been gone for the longest (and are more likely to return soon).
*'''Delta''': For the Spirit's first visit, it's the approximate number of 2-week gaps between that first appearance and the end of the Spirit's Season. For subsequent visits, it's the gap between the previous and current visit. For example, Daydream Forester's Delta value of &Delta;23 is calculated by taking its current visit number (TS#60) and subtracting 37 (Season of Assembly's end date of June 13, 2021 coincides with when TS#37 left). Error visits are ignored in this calculation.

===Visit Statistics===
When Spirits visit after their first time, this gives players who missed out during previous visits a chance to catch up. Second and onward visits do not increase maximum [[Winged Light]]. This means that when a Spirit visits again as a Traveling Spirit or in a group of Returning Spirits, players can get closer to the maximum possible Winged Light. The latest start allowing [[Winged Light#Maximum Possible Winged Light|Maximum Winged Light]] is the 64th Traveling Spirit on June 23, 2022 until [[Peeking Postman]] revisits and will then be the 72nd Traveling Spirit on October 13, 2022. Maximum Winged Light from Spirit visits is currently '''{{Game Stat|wings_buff_ts}}'''. This number only doesn't correspond to the number of '''unique''' visits due to the fact that [[Ancient Light]] and [[Ancient Darkness]] from Season of Shattering are counted as 2 Entities instead of 4, although in total they have 4 Friendship Trees and provide 4 [[Wing Buffs]].
