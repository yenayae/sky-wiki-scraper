The '''Season of Rhythm''' began Friday, January 24, 2020 and ended on Sunday, April 5, 2020, lasting 72 days. It was the fourth seasonal event in Sky since the game's global release.

This season brought revamped Spirits and Cosmetics from the [[Winter 2018]] season that appeared in Pre-Release versions of the game, as well as some new collectibles. [[Home]] was given a snowy makeover with an ice rink and [[Traveling Spirits]] were introduced to the game.

The Season of Rhythm followed the [[Season of Belonging]], and preceded the [[Season of Enchantment]].<br>

==Trivia== 
*The promotional artwork for the Season of Rhythm is an altered version of the one for the [[Winter 2018]] Season. The additions include the duck mask on the [[Respectful Pianist]] and the Ultimate Gift hair on the [[Thoughtful Director]]. The [[Troupe Juggler]] and [[Festival Spin Dancer]]'s masks also feature the outdated chevron-shaped marks compared the triangular ones that were used in Live version of the Season of Rhythm.
*The Guide in [[Sky Beta|Beta]] was different from the Live version. The Guide in Beta had the [[Ushering Stargazer]] Spirit body model, wore a mask similar to the [[Doublefive Light Catcher]] from the [[Season of Lightseekers]] with chevron-shaped marks, had the [[Hide'n'Seek Pioneer]] hair, and carried the Winter Piano.<ref>As seen in  Tara's Season of Rhythm Beta video: https://youtu.be/7-hqBLGesS0&t=301</ref>
