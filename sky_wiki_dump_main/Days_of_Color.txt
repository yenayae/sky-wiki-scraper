<section begin="Summary" />'''Days of Color''' (formerly known as '''Days of Rainbow''') is an annual charity event celebrating diversity, unity, and hope that usually happens around mid-June and lasts until the beginning of July, loosely coinciding with [[wp:LGBT pride|Pride Month]]. As part of the celebrations, ''Sky'' encourages players to make a difference in the real world by partnering with charities that champion equality and support marginalized communities, to whom they donate part of the net profit from [[IAP]] sales.<section end="Summary" />

During the event, rainbows are added to the skies of [[Home]] and throughout the [[Realms]] with two types of rainbow-themed [[Quests#Days of Color|Daily Quests]]. Players can also find a puzzle involving [[Days of Color/2023#Cape Color Categorization|Cape colors]] and a special geyser that grants colorful [[Spells]] in the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. Completing this puzzle unlocks access to a special location high above the clouds—and rewards a bit of [[Pieces of Light|Light]] in the process.

Rainbow-themed IAPs are available from the in-game shop, and the Color Guide Spirit offers various cosmetics from their Friendship Tree. Free [[Spells]] to try all of these items (alongside [[Days of Color/2020#Rainbow Spell|Rainbow]] and [[Days of Color/2021#Trail Spells|Trail Spells]]) can also be collected from the [[Spell Shops#Sleepy Traveler's Boat|Sleepy Traveler's boat]] at Home, the [[Aviary Village#Spell Shop|Spell Shop]] in Aviary Village, or the Color Gift Box found at the event area.

==[[Days of Color/2025|May 2025]]==
{{#ev:youtube|PstqxC1aZTE||right|Days of Color 2025 Trailer}}
Days of Color returned for a sixth year! The event began on '''Monday, May 26, 2025 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and continued until '''Sunday, June 8, 2025 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''14''' days. Rainbows brightened the skies above the [[Daylight Prairie#Prairie Village|Prairie Village]], with new games, a colorful race, and more! For the duration of the event, the Color Guide stood at [[Home]] and [[Aviary Village]], offering to teleport players to the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area|Daylight Prairie's Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. 

Sky continued its partnership to support the Trevor Project, a leading suicide prevention and mental health organization for LGBTQ youth, providing crisis services, education, and peer support to help young people thrive. Between five ({{Cost|5.00 USD|refnote}}) and ten dollars ({{Cost|10.00 USD|refnote}}) were donated on behalf of each new and returning [[Premium Candle Shop|IAP]] purchases from this event.

===Cape Color Categorization===
<!-- image of the color puzzle -->
Across the two weeks of the event, players can participate in a puzzle involving [[Cape]] colors in the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. Fully charge the bridges of light surrounding the elevator platform by wearing a Cape that matches their color and standing next to their representative [[Spirits]]. Players can use the nearby Cape [[Cosmetics#Outfit Shrines|Outfit Shrine]] to quickly change their equipped Cape or take a dip in the area's special geyser to receive a random Cape [[Spell]].

==[[Days of Color/2022|June 2022]]==
[[File:Days_of_Rainbow_2022.png|thumb|250x250px|Promotional image - Days of Rainbow 2022]]
Days of Rainbow returned for its third year! The event began on '''Thursday,''' '''June 30, 2022 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and ended on '''Wednesday, July 13, 2022 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''14''' days. Event Guide Spirits and fellow players celebrated the brilliant mosaic that brings life to the world of Sky.

Behind the two Guides at Home, over the water, was a double rainbow that slowly spawned [[Pieces of Light]]. Each piece of light was a different color and briefly changed your trail into the corresponding color. Large floating Jellyfish around the double rainbow were available to help players recharge.

==Trivia==
*Days of Rainbow 2020 was not available in the [[NetEase|Chinese version]] of ''Sky'': instead, the Days of Dragon Boat took its place. In 2021, Days of Rainbow was introduced in the Chinese version of ''Sky'', renamed 缤纷飞行日 (roughly translates to "Colorful Flight Day"), from November 18 to December 1, 2021. The Chinese version does not include the [[Days of Color/2021#Rainbow Pack|Rainbow Pack]] IAP.
* Although [[wp:Rainbow#Double_rainbows|double rainbows]] do really occur in nature, the double rainbow that appears at [[Home]] during the event is not realistic. In real double rainbows, the colors of the outer rainbow are reversed (red on the inside of the arc and violet on the outside).
