The first Sky Anniversary celebration started on '''July 13, 2020 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' (coinciding with the start of [[Season of Sanctuary]]) and ended on '''July 19, 2020 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''7''' days. A [[Double_Currency_Events#Anniversary Candle Event - July 2020|Double Currency Event]] also took place. A meditation [[Quests|quest]] for the Meditation Shrine near the [[Secret Area|Office]] door was added.

===2020 New Cosmetics and Props===

====1 Year Anniversary Hat====
The 1 Year Anniversary Hat is a light blue cone party hat. This [[Hair Accessory]] was introduced in 2020 for {{Cost|3 H}} from the Anniversary  Guide. It was only available during Sky's 2020 Anniversary.
