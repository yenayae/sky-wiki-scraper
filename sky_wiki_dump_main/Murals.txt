Murals are found all across the realms depicting the lore of Sky. Each mural is labeled with what type they are (Glowing, Dim, Carving), what color they are (Gray, White, Yellow, Blue), what triggers them to appear or to be completed (Candles, Quest, Proximity, Memory) and the main depicted elements (Creatures, Symbols, Diamond, Spirits, Elder, Boat, Constellation, Temple, Skykid, Eden Elder, Spherelight, Colorlights, Environmental, Functionality).

Compilation, descriptions, and main screenshot credit to salemango on Discord. A Retconned, some Isle, some Vault screenshots taken by eclipsethecoyote on Discord, the Office Portal and some Treasure Reef screenshots taken by alasdair_pl on Discord and some Isle screenshots taken by vela_pulsar on Discord. Transferring information to wiki and some missing Forgotten Ark screenshots taken by Ray808080.
