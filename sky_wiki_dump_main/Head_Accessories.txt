'''Head Accessories''' are [[Cosmetics]] that are worn on the head in addition to any selected [[Hair Accessories]]. This includes items such as earrings, horns or antlers, and earmuffs, with the majority only obtainable as an [[IAP]]. Head Accessories share the middle [[Outfit Shrine]] with [[Hair]], where they are listed in the third tab (the horns {{Icon| Icon hair dreams hermit horns.png | label= }} icon). 

Head Accessories were first introduced in the game with Patch [[Update:Live_0.27.0_(294170_Android,_Huawei)_(292054_iOS,_PlayStation,_Steam,_Switch)|0.27.0]] in October 2024, recategorizing multiple existing [[Face Accessories]] and further expanding the customization options available to players after the first the [[Cosmetics#Types of Cosmetics|Closet]] category expansion in Patch {{v|0.22.0}}.

==Headphones==
A special Head Accessory <ref>After the [[Outfit Shrine|Closet]] category expansion in Patch {{v|0.22.0}}, several Cosmetics were recategorized, including the Headphones. At that time, the headphones were considered a Face Accessory instead of a [[Hair Accessory]]. After Patch {{v|0.27.0}} some Cosmetics, including the Headphones, were again recategorized into a new Head Accessories section.</ref>, only available when playing on IOS devices, will appear when external audio outside of Sky â€“ e.g. Apple Music, YouTube, Spotify, a phone call, etc. - plays. This item will only appear if the player is not wearing any other Head Accessory<ref>Before the [[Update:Live_0.12.2_(160278)|January 19, 2021 Hot Fix]], the Headphones would override other Head Accessories.</ref>. The Headphones are a pair of white rings with three spikes, a yellow accent ring which glows in the dark, and a white, four-pointed star placed in the center, attached to a beige headband with an orange interior.

*The game's background music will be muted while there is external audio
*There is no notification once it's on or removed
*The Headphones will disappear once the external audio is stopped
*Does not work on Macs with M1 chip or later

<small>There is no icon associated with this Cosmetic, as it can not be selected in the Outfit Shrine.</small><br>
