<section begin="Summary" />'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Shattering]] who offered quests and [[The Void of Shattering#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. {{PAGENAME}} was available at Home during the Season. It is now only accessible on days with '''Red''' [[Shard Eruptions]] after cleansing the Darkness and meditating to enter the Ancient Memory. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item||necklace_u|link=#Shattering Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item||cape_m|label=Shattering Ultimate Manta Cape|link=#Shattering Ultimate Manta Cape|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item||cape_k|label=Shattering Ultimate Krill Cape|link=#Shattering Ultimate Krill Cape|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<section end="Summary" />

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Shattering Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Shattering Ultimate [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Void of Shattering Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Shattering only.

===Shattering Ultimate Manta Cape===
The Shattering Ultimate Manta [[Cape]] is white and in the shape of a [[manta]], with golden accents around the tail, wings and neck. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Season Heart|2|season=Shattering}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

===Shattering Ultimate Krill Cape===
The Shattering Ultimate Krill [[Cape]] is black and spiky, in the shape similar to a [[Krill]] with bat-like wings, a spiky tail and a fluffy neckpiece. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Season Heart|2|season=Shattering}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>

==Quest Tree==
The Void of Shattering Guide's Quest Tree is available for all players to complete quests from the [[Season of Shattering]]. Once you completed a quest, a heart was available to be collected for free during the Season of Shattering only. Since the end of the Season, players completing the Quests for the first time have to pay {{Candle|3}} to obtain these Hearts. Players can only access the Guide in the Ancient Memory on days with '''Red''' [[Shard Eruptions]].
