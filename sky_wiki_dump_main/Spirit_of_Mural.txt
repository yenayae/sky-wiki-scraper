<section begin="Summary" />The '''Spirit of Mural''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer]] who offers Quests and [[Spirit of Mural#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Spirit of Mural is found at the [[Crescent Oasis]], in front of the [[Murals|Mural]] by the pool of water. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|necklace_u|link=Spirit of Mural#Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Pendant|label=Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|outfit_u|link=Spirit of Mural#Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Outfit|label=Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Outfit|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|hair_u|link=Spirit of Mural#Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Hair|label=Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Hair|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|cape_u|link=Spirit of Mural#Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Cape|label=Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Cape|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders. This Ultimate Guide also provides new cosmetics in the Quest Tree, available to all players: a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|mask|link=Spirit of Mural#Mask|inline_text}} and a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Mural|hairpiece|link=Spirit of Mural#Hair Accessory|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace, but colored silver instead of gold. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Spirit of Mural offered 3 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season_Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of the Nine-Colored Deer only.

===<span id="Ultimate Outfit">Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Outfit</span>===
The Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate [[Outfit]] is a pair of red, wide-legged pants with a jagged teal and beige pattern at the cuffs, a pair of teal loops hanging from the waist, and a white, short-sleeved top tucked into the waistband. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Nine-Colored Deer}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Hair">Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Hair</span>===
The Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate [[Hair]] is a loosely pulled back bun that resembles the shape of rabbit ears, tied with a thin red ribbon and decorated with a small, tasseled blue gem in the front. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|1 SH|season=Nine-Colored Deer}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Cape">Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate Cape</span>===
The Nine-Colored Deer Ultimate [[Cape]] is a transparent sparkly blue sash that floats over the player's shoulders, accompanied by a short transparent sparkly yellow shawl with rings that connect the two in the front. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Nine-Colored Deer}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node.<br>

==Additional Cosmetics==
Upon completing the [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer#Quest #3|third]] and [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer#Quest #5|fifth Quests]] of the Season of the Nine-Colored Deer, two additional cosmetics were available to collect for regular Candles through the Spirit of Mural's Friendship Tree. No [[Season Pass]] or other IAP purchase were necessary for these collaboration items. The themed Mask offered for the Nine-Colored Deer collaboration was available until the Season ended on '''Sunday, March 31, 2024 at 23:59 PDT (UTC -7)''', while the themed Hair Accessory is '''permanently available''' from the Season Guide.

Please note that since it is part of a limited collaboration, the Mask will not be available once the Season is over and is not scheduled to return in future years.

===<span id="Mask">Mask</span>===
<section begin="Deer Mask" />
The [[Mask]] offered by the Spirit of Mural is white with a protruding snout and multi-colored markings around the eyes that resemble those of the Nine-Colored Deer, costing {{Cost|120 C}}. It was available to all players after completing [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer#Quest 5|Quest #5]] of the Season of the Nine-Colored Deer, from the Quest Tree's seventh node. <small>(Since these are part of a limited collaboration, they are not scheduled to return in future years.)</small><br>

===<span id="Hair Accessory">Hair Accessory</span>===
<section begin="Petal Accessory" />
The [[Hair Accessory]] offered by the Spirit of Mural is a pale blue flower with three petals that sits on the side of the player's head, costing {{Cost|50 C}}. It is permanently available to all players after completing [[Season of the Nine-Colored Deer#Quest 3|Quest #3]] of the Season of the Nine-Colored Deer, from the Quest Tree's fourth node.<br>

==Personality==

* 🔸 Often entrusted as a confidante
* 🔸 Observes at least as much as they speak
* 🔸 Has a sharp memory for details
* 🔸 Knows by heart which creatures arrive at the oasis when
