<section begin="Summary" />The '''Vault Elder's Lantern''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of The Two Embers - Part 1]] who offers Quests and [[Vault Elder's Lantern#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Vault Elder's Lantern is found in the Sky Cinema at [[Aviary Village]], in the room to the left of the shop spirits and opposite the wardrobe room. The Ultimate Guide offers a {{Spirit Item|Vault Elder's Lantern|necklace_u|inline_text|link=Vault Elder's Lantern#Seasonal Pendant}}, a {{Spirit Item|Vault Elder's Lantern|hairpiece_u|inline_text|link=Vault Elder's Lantern#Ultimate Hairpiece}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Vault Elder's Lantern|cape_u|inline_text|link=Vault Elder's Lantern#Ultimate Cape}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.

This Ultimate Guide also provides a new cosmetic in their Quest Tree, permanently available to all players: a {{Spirit Item|Vault Elder's Lantern|prop_a|inline_text|link=Vault Elder's Lantern#Prop}}.<section end="Summary" /><br><br>

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Two Embers Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Two Embers Ultimate [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace, with two faces, one silver and one gold. It is a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Two Embers offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Two Embers - Part 1 only.

===<span id="Ultimate Hairpiece">Two Embers Part 1 Ultimate Hair Accessory</span>===
The Two Embers Ultimate [[Hair Accessory]] consists of glowing blue shapes and stars that portray the [[Vault of Knowledge]]'s [[Constellation]] and float behind the player's head. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Two Embers Part 1}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Cape">Two Embers Part 1 Ultimate Cape</span>===
The Two Embers Ultimate [[Cape]] consists of a white overcoat and a purple stole. Both parts are decorated with ornaments, mostly consisting of golden triangle-shapes at the hems. The interior and the stole have stars sprinkled on them that sparkle brightly. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Two Embers Part 1}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.

This item gives the wearer and the friends they hold hands with the special ability to fly in [[The Last City]], as well as grant access to the Season's special area within it, the [[The Last City#Cloister|Cloister]]. The entrance can be found near the back of the city, opposite of the Manatee pens. This ability was added in Patch {{v|0.30.5}}.

==Additional Collectibles==
Upon completing [[Season of The Two Embers - Part 1#Quest #5|Quest #5]] of the Season of The Two Embers - Part 1, one additional cosmetic is available to collect for {{Cost|C}} [[Currency#Candles|regular Candles]] through the Vault Elder Lantern's [[#Quest Tree|Quest Tree]]. No [[Season Pass]] or other [[IAP]] purchase is necessary for this collaboration item.

===Prop===
The [[Prop]] offered by the Vault Elder's Lantern is a poster of [[Two Embers]] series, like the ones seen in the hallway of the [[Sky Anniversary#Sky Cinema|Sky Cinema]], costing {{Cost|27 C}}. It depicts the main character of the series, holding the Manatee, with the Eden mountain in the background—the poster is surrounded by a stone frame. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as a blue bag with a yellow drawstring. 

It is '''permanently available''' to all players after completing [[Season of The Two Embers - Part 1#Quest #5|Quest #5]] of the Season of The Two Embers - Part 1, after unlocking the Quest Tree's fifth node.
