The second year of Days of Rainbow began on '''Monday, June 14, 2021 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and concluded on '''Sunday, June 27, 2021 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting for a total of '''14''' days. The Days of Rainbow Guide appeared at [[Home]] for the duration of the event, offering rainbow-themed cosmetics and [[Days of Color#Trails Spells|Trails Spells]], seven costing {{Cost|5 C}} and six costing {{Cost|10 C}}. Free temporary [[Spells]] were also available for all cosmetics from the [[Sleepy Traveling Merchant]]'s boat.

===2021 New Cosmetics and Props===
====Rainbow Pack<span id="Rainbow Hair Flower"></span>====
The Rainbow Pack contains two items: the Rainbow Flower and {{Cost|75 C}}. The Rainbow Flower is a small flower with wide, rainbow-colored petals that sits on the left side of the player's head. This [[Hair Accessory]] was introduced in 2021 and was available for {{Cost|19.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event, during which '''100% of the net proceeds went to charity''' (specifically, '''[https://www.thetrevorproject.org/ The Trevor Project]''' and '''[https://www.globalfundforwomen.org/ The Global Fund for Women]''').

Notably, the Rainbow Pack did not return alongside other past items for [[#2022|Days of Rainbow 2022]], with the item only available that year as a free [[Spell]].<br>

====Rainbow Hat<span id="Rainbow Beanie Hat"></span>====
::'''''"Stays cool on the warmest of days."'''''
The Rainbow Hat is a short wavy hairstyle almost entirely covered by a beanie with soft rainbow stripes and a pale blue headband. This [[Hair]] was introduced in 2021 and was available for {{Cost|9.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event. Note that this item is considered a Hair, not a [[Hair Accessory]], and as such it can not be worn over other Hairstyles.<br>

====Rainbow Braid Accessory<span id="Rainbow Braid"></span>====
The Rainbow Braid Accessory is a tassel with a soft rainbow gradient and a tuft of hair at the end that's attached to the right side of the player's head. This [[Head Accessory]] was introduced in 2021 and was available for {{Cost|20 H}} from the Days of Rainbow Guide at [[Home]] for the duration of the event.<br>

====Rainbow Cape====
The Rainbow Cape is a pale blue version of the [[Capes#Base Capes|Base Cape]] with a soft rainbow gradient along the bottom edge. This [[Cape]] was introduced in 2021 and was available for {{Cost|175 C}} from the Days of Rainbow Guide at [[Home]] for the duration of the event.<br>

===2021 New Spells===
====Trail Spells====
Starting in 2021, multiple colored [[Spells#Trail Spells|Trail Spells]] are available during Days of Color. Purchasing one of these [[Spells]] gives the player a '''randomly-selected''' Trail Spell. The length of the Spell is 1 hour, 2 hours, or 3 hours, depending upon which randomly-selected color is received.

The standard cost of a Trail Spell is {{Cost|5 C}}<ref name="10_candle_trail_spell">In 2021, the cost of a trail spell went up to {{Cost|10 C}} on the eighth spell purchased. The more expensive spell was identical to the cheaper version.</ref>. A limited number of Spells are available from the Color Guide's Friendship Tree. Additional Spells are available from the [[Spell Shop]] (either at [[Forgotten Ark]] or the [[Sleepy Traveling Merchant]]).
