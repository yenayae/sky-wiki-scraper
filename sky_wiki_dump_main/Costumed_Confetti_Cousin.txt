<section begin="Summary" />The Costumed Confetti Cousin is a [[Seasonal Spirit]] from the [[Season of the Blue Bird]]. The Costumed Confetti Cousin is found at the Blue Bird Theater in the Elevated Clearing from [[Hidden Forest]]. The collectibles [[#Traveling Spirit|offered]] by this Spirit are an {{Spirit Item|Costumed Confetti Cousin|Outfit|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Costumed Confetti Cousin|Mask|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Costumed Confetti Cousin|Hair|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Costumed Confetti Cousin|Hairpiece|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Spirit is a red knee-length dress with high-collared undershirt, white stockings, and red shoes. It features puffed sleeves, a V-shaped waistband, and diamond decorations hanging off the hem. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|69 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total). <!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br>

===<span id="Mask">Mask</span>===
The [[Mask]] offered by this Spirit is a teal, bird-themed masquerade mask. The top, bottom, and eyes are trimmed in silver, and it features a small pointed beak and forehead diamond. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|27 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br>

===<span id="Hair">Hair</span>===
The [[Hair]] offered by this Spirit is a wide-brimmed red hat with a round bob underneath. The hat has a silver rim and band, and diamond with a large feather. It was available to all players for {{Cost|25 SC|season=Blue Bird}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fifth node ({{Cost|94 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br>
