__NOTOC__
This page compiles all of the text '''hints''' released by TGC that tease the identity of upcoming [[Traveling Spirits]]. Due to changes in the way Traveling Spirits are revealed as of January 2024, Spirits are no longer expected to receive text hints for the foreseeable future.<ref name="End of text hints">On April 11, 2024, TGC Concept Artist Ash announced on the official Discord server that they have [https://discord.com/channels/575762611111592007/575827782144098304/1228033339911569428 retired from providing text hints] with the introduction of the [https://discord.com/channels/575762611111592007/575827782144098304/1228034005329383477 new photo hints].</ref> Click the button below to reveal all the provided hints on this page or visit the Spirits' individual pages.
