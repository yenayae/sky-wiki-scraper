'''Mask''' is a general term in Sky for any [[Cosmetics]] that alter the appearance of the player's face. This includes full-face masks (including multiple animal-themed masks), scarves, beards and other facial hair, and accessories such as blindfolds. One "Mask" (the [[#Proud Victor|Proud Victor]]'s) is technically a non-mask; you just see the character's underlying face.

The current Mask is selected in the second [[Outfit Shrine]] from the left, in the first tab (with the Base Mask {{Icon| Icon_mask_default.png }} icon). The second tab in the Mask Closet (the sunglasses {{Spirit Item|Chill Sunbather|facepiece}} icon) lists [[Face Accessories]] while the third tab (the bowtie {{Spirit Item|Comfort of Kindness|neckpiece}} icon) lists [[Necklaces]]. One Necklace, one, Face Accessory, and one Mask can be worn simultaneously but all three can be worn individually as well.
{{clear|left}}
==Base Mask==
This is the Mask worn by the player at the start of the game. Note that this is in fact a Mask, and is not just the avatar's underlying face. The [[#Proud Victor|Proud Victor]]'s Mask is the only non-mask, that is also bare-faced.<br>

==Special Masks==
In general, the only effect of changing your Mask is the the appearance of your face. However, there are some Masks with special functions. 
* The [[#Hide'n'Seek Pioneer|Chibi Mask]] shrinks your entire body, like a shrinking Spell. 
* The [[Sky for PlayStation#Journey Pack|Journey Pack]] Mask grants a unique Journey-inspired voice when worn. To hear the Journey-inspired voice, honk with the default Call equipped.
* Both the [[Abyss Guide#Oxygen Mask|oxygen Mask]] and the [[Abyss Guide#Ultimate Mask|Season of Abyss Ultimate Mask]] give players an additional 15 seconds of oxygen when underwater.
