<section begin="Summary" />The Woodcutting Pleaful Parent is a [[Seasonal Spirit]] from the [[Season of the Blue Bird]]. The Woodcutting Pleaful Parent is found at the Blue Bird Theater in the Elevated Clearing from [[Hidden Forest]].  The collectibles [[#Traveling Spirit|offered]] by this Spirit are an {{Spirit Item|Woodcutting Pleaful Parent|Outfit|inline_text}}, a pair of {{Spirit Item|Woodcutting Pleaful Parent|Shoes|inline_text}}, 
and the {{Music Sheet|43|inline_text}}.

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Outfit">Outfit</span>===
The [[Outfit]] offered by this Spirit is a gloved shirt and brown balloon pants with suspenders. The Y-shaped suspenders feature triangular ornaments on the front of each strap. Silver curls decorate the glove cuffs and the pants' sides. It was available to all players for {{Cost|23 SC|season=Blue Bird}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|68 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<!-- Thanks Oz (ozotronic for item description--><br><br>

===<span id="Shoes">Shoes</span>===
The [[Shoes]] offered by this Spirit are large gray-brown boots with a V-shaped front that is embellished with curled, silver lining. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|27 SC|season=Blue Bird}} total).<br><br>
