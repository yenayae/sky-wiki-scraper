<section begin="Summary" />The '''Sanctuary Guide''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Sanctuary]] who offers Quests and [[#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Sanctuary Guide is found on the left side of the [[Sanctuary Islands]], next to the large bells monument. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item||necklace_u|link=Sanctuary Guide#Sanctuary Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item||cape_u|link=Sanctuary Guide#Sanctuary Ultimate Cape|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item||instrument_u|link=Sanctuary Guide#Sanctuary Ultimate Instrument|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<section end="Summary" />

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Sanctuary Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Sanctuary Seasonal [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the [[#Friendship Tree|Friendship Tree]]'s first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Sanctuary Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Hearts|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Sanctuary only.

===<span id="Ultimate Cape">Sanctuary Ultimate Cape</span>===
The Sanctuary Ultimate [[Cape]] is white with yellow accents curving along the top and bottom hems, overall resembling the shape of a [[Mantas|Manta]]. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item, available to purchase for {{Season Heart|3|season=Sanctuary}} Seasonal Hearts after unlocking the [[#Friendship Tree|Friendship Tree]]'s second node.<br>

===<span id="Ultimate Instrument">Sanctuary Ultimate Instrument</span>===
The Sanctuary Ultimate [[Instrument]] is a metallic blue handpan with white accents. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available to purchase for {{Season Heart|3|season=Sanctuary}} Seasonal Hearts, after unlocking the [[#Friendship Tree|Friendship Tree]]'s first node. When playing the Sanctuary Handpan, the player is sitting in a cross-legged position.

{{#lst:Harmony_Hall|handpan_notes}}
Since the end of the [[Season of Sanctuary|Season]], the Sanctuary Handpan is not for available for purchase anymore, nor is it to be found in [[Harmony Hall]]. However, a variant, the [[Triumph Handpan]] was introduced with [[Harmony Hall]]. The sound is similar, but the Triumph Handpan has a less rich and more hollow sound than the Sanctuary version. The visuals are also different.<br>

==Quest Tree==
The Sanctuary Guide Quest Tree is available for all players to complete Quests from the Sanctuary Guide. During the [[Season of Sanctuary]], the [[Currency#Hearts|Hearts]] awarded for completing each Quest were free. Since the end of the Season, players completing the Quests for the first time have to pay {{Candle|3}} to obtain these Hearts.
