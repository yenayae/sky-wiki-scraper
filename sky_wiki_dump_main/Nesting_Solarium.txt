<section begin="Summary" />The '''Nesting Solarium''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Nesting]]. The Nesting Solarium was found within the [[Nesting Workshop]] in [[Aviary Village]], marked outside by a sign with Nesting's emblem. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Solarium|prop_t|inline_text|link=Nesting Solarium#Table|label=Table Prop}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Solarium|prop_b|inline_text|link=Nesting Solarium#Bathtub|label=Bathtub Prop}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Solarium|prop_h|inline_text|link=Nesting Solarium#Hanging Planter|label=Hanging Planter Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Solarium|prop_p|inline_text|link=Nesting Solarium#Painting|label=Painting Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of four [[Props]]: a [[#Table|Table]], a [[#Bathtub|Bathtub]], a [[#Hanging Planter|Hanging Planter]], and a [[#Painting|Painting]].

====Table====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a table made from a pale tree stump and decorated with a small yellow placemat, a potted plant, and two white mugs. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and allows two players to stand and drink from. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|34 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|100 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Bathtub====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a water-filled bathtub made from a pale, hollowed out tree stump with a yellow towel hanging from a branch extended from its side. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and seats a single player. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|66 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Hanging Planter====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a small hanging planter made of pale wood that contains a non-descript pale green plant. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|22 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|36 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Painting====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a large painting with a pale wood frame that depicts the [[Nesting Guide]] standing with the [[Elder of the Forest]] in a wooded area. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|14 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>
