<section begin="Summary" />The '''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a Seasonal Guide (also known as Quest Giver) from the [[Season of Flight]] who offers quests and [[Flight Guide#Ultimate Gifts|cosmetics]]. The Flight Guide is found on the main Island in the [[Wind Paths]] area. The Ultimate Guide offered a {{Spirit Item||necklace_u|link=#Flight Ultimate Pendant|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item||outfit_u|link=#Flight Ultimate Outfit|inline_text}}, and a {{Spirit Item||Hairpiece_u|link=#Flight Ultimate Hair Accessory|inline_text}} for [[Season Pass]] holders.<section end="Summary" /><br>

==<span id="Ultimate Pendant"><span id="Flight Ultimate Pendant">Seasonal Pendant</span></span>==
The Flight Ultimate [[Necklaces|Pendant]] is the usual gold pendant featuring the Season's emblem attached to a necklace. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for free from the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Ultimate Gifts==
The Season of Flight Guide offered 2 Ultimate Gifts, available to be purchased by [[Season Pass]] holders with [[Currency#Season Heart|Seasonal Hearts]] during the Season of Flight only.

===Flight Ultimate Outfit===
The Flight Ultimate [[Outfit]] has a top with blue sleeves, a brown rope belt, and a blue pattered piece of fabric hanging down the torso, as well as simple brown pants and boots. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Flight}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node.<br>{{Dye_Details

===Flight Ultimate Hair Accessory===
The Flight Ultimate [[Hair Accessory]] features two feather-shaped accessories, in white, blue and yellow, that the player wears on either side of their head. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available for {{Cost|2 SH|season=Flight}} after unlocking the Friendship Tree's first node.<br>

==Quest Tree==
The Flight Guide Quest Tree is available for all players to complete quests from the [[Season of Flight]]. Once you completed a quest, a heart was available to be collected for free during the Season of Flight only. Since the end of the Season, players completing the Quests for the first time have to pay {{Candle|3}} to obtain these Hearts.
