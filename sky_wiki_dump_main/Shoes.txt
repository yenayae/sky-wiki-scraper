'''Shoes''' are [[Cosmetics]] that alter the appearance of the player's feet. They come in a variety of styles and designs and can be paired with any [[Outfit]] you choose, excluding those that already include footwear. (''To see what can and cannot be worn with Shoes, view the availability table on the [[Outfits#Available_Outfits|Outfit]] page.'') Shoes share the left-most [[Outfit Shrine]] with Outfits, where they are listed in the second tab (the boot {{Spirit Item|Chuckling Scout|footwear}} icon).

Shoes were first introduced in the game with Patch {{v|0.22.0}} in July 2023. For players that have the [[Days of Mischief#Mischief Witch Jumper|Mischief Witch Jumper]], the Season of Remembrance’s [[Pleading Child]] Outfit, or the Season of Assembly’s [[Chuckling Scout]] Outfit, the Shoes that were included with the Outfit are now found within a separate tab in the Outfit Shrine. With Patch {{v|0.24.5}} in February 2024, the [[Peeking Postman]] Outfit also received this change. 
{{clear}}
==Base Shoes==
This is the footwear worn by the player at the start of the game. These are not actually Shoes, but instead the player's bare feet.
