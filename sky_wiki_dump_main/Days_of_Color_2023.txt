Starting with this year, Days of Rainbow was '''renamed''' to "Days of Color". The most colorful time of Sky’s year began on '''Thursday, June 1, 2023 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' and ran until  '''Wednesday, June 14, 2023 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', lasting '''14''' days. The Days of Color Guide arrived in [[Home]] for the duration of the event, and offered to teleport players to where the main festivities were held: the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area|secretive area]] high above Daylight Prairie, expanded specially for the occasion.

Once in the event area, players with the corresponding [[Cape]] color could charge the eight colorful platforms (which became available one by one each day of the event). From the eighth day onward, all of the platforms could be charged with the aid of other players. For help with Cape colors, use the [[Capes#Available Capes|Available Capes table on the Capes page]]. Once all of them were charged, a cutscene would play showing that the center of the puzzle has activated, forming a large stream of light which propelled players to the floating islands above, along with making the beams generate colorful wax.

Beginning with this event, a new currency was introduced in the form of "Event Currency", which are used to purchase event-exclusive items. (''For more detailed information, see the [[Currency#Event Currency|Currency]] page.'') The total number of bubble-shaped Tickets available each day varied:

*Every day:
**{{Cost|4 T|event=color}} hidden throughout the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]] in Daylight Prairie (see visual guide)
**{{Cost|4 T|event=color}} from completing the rainbow puzzle in the event area, by activating all the available color bridges

*Days with a [[Quests#Days of Color|Rainbow Daily Quest]]:
**{{Cost|4 T|event=color}} at the end of the Daily Quest rainbow

*Weekends (Friday - Sunday):
**{{Cost|2 T|event=color}} next to the [[Timing System#In-Game Clocks|Rainbow Clock]] in Hidden Forest

Exclusively on weekends (Friday - Sunday), players could also buy up to 7 additional Tickets (costing {{Cost|2 AC|event=color}} each) from the Color Gift Box next to the Color Guides in the event area.{{Clear|right}}

===Cape Color Categorization===
<!-- image of the color puzzle -->
Across the two weeks of the event, players can participate in a puzzle involving [[Cape]] colors in the [[Eight-Player Puzzle Area]]. Fully charge the bridges of light surrounding the elevator platform by wearing a Cape that matches their color and standing next to their representative [[Spirits]]. Players can use the nearby Cape [[Cosmetics#Outfit Shrines|Outfit Shrine]] to quickly change their equipped Cape or take a dip in the area's special geyser to receive a random Cape [[Spell]].

This [[Outfit]] was introduced in 2023 and was available for {{Cost|14.99 USD|refnote}} from the [[Premium Candle Shop]] for the duration of the event.<br>
