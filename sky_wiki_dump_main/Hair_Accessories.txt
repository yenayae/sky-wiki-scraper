'''Hair Accessories''' (sometimes called '''Headpieces''') are [[Cosmetics]] that are worn on the head in addition to any selected [[Hair]]. Hair Accessories share the middle [[Outfit Shrine]] with Hair, where the Hair Accessories are found in the second tab (the hat {{Icon| Icon hair summer hat headpiece.png | label= }} icon). 

Even though Hair Accessories are identified using a hat icon, less than a quarter of the Hair Accessories are hats; most of them are small decorative add-ons like flowers. Most of the hats in the game are actually considered to be [[Hair]] (meaning that they replace any previously selected hairstyle). All Hair Accessories are worn in addition to the Hairstyle.

Hair Accessories are overall harder to obtain than most types of Cosmetics. None are available from [[Regular Spirits]]; many are Ultimate Gifts or limited-time offers.
