<section begin="Summary" />The '''Spirit of Adventure''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Moomin]]. The Spirit of Adventure is found on a grassy mound to the right in [[Moominvalley Glade]]. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|Spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Adventure|Hair|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Adventure|Cape|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Adventure|Instrument|inline_text|label=Harmonica Instrument}}, a {{Spirit Item|Spirit of Adventure|Prop|inline_text|label=Snufkin Tent Prop}}, and {{Music Sheet|41|inline_text}}.<section end="Summary" /><br>

===<span id="Harmonica">Instrument</span>===
The [[Instrument]] offered by this Shop is the '''Harmonica'''<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It has a gray rectangular body and is decorated by a lighter gray {{Season Icon|Belonging|inline_text}} symbol. When carried on its owner's back, the Instrument appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|20 SC|season=Moomin}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's second node. The player is standing while playing the Instrument, which plays 15 notes.

The Harmonica plays the [[Music Sheet Parts|wind part]] on Music Sheets. Its [[Instrument Pitch|pitch]] is the same as the standard [[Piano]] (in C major, it covers C<sub>4</sub> to C<sub>6</sub>).<br>

===<span id="Prop">Prop</span>===
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a tall, conical green tent with light green accents<!--, costing {{Cost| ??? C}} from the Traveling Spirit-->. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. Once placed, the player can enter the tent and crawl around inside on their elbows and knees. When carried on its owner's back, it appears as an oversized brown bag with a rolled-up green canvas secured beneath the top flap. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|58 SC|season=Moomin}} total).<br>
