<section begin="Summary" />The '''Nesting Nook''' is a [[Ancestors#Seasonal Entities|Seasonal Shop]] from the [[Season of Nesting]]. The Nesting Nook was found within the [[Nesting Workshop]] in [[Aviary Village]], marked outside by a sign with Nesting's emblem. Unlike typical [[Seasonal Spirits]], there is no need to first relive a [[Spirit Memories|spirit memory]] to access this Shop and it does not offer an [[Expression]]. The collectibles offered by this Shop are a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Nook|Hairpiece|inline_text}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Nook|prop_c|inline_text|link=Nesting Nook#Couch|label=Couch Prop}}, a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Nook|prop_s|inline_text|link=Nesting Nook#Shelf|label=Shelf Prop}}, and a {{Spirit Item|Nesting Nook|prop_r|inline_text|link=Nesting Nook#Spice Rack|label=Spice Rack Prop}}.<section end="Summary" />

==Cosmetics==
===<span id="Hair Accessory">Hair Accessory</span>===
The [[Hair Accessory]] offered by this Shop is a chunky yellow pencil placed behind the player's left ear. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's fourth node ({{Cost|92 SC|season=Nesting}} total).

===<span id="Prop">Props</span>===
This Seasonal Shop offers a total of three [[Props]]: a [[#Couch|Couch]], a [[#Shelf|Shelf]], and a [[#Spice Rack|Spice Rack]].

====Couch====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a double-seater couch made of pale wood, featuring a curved back and three yellow pillows on its seats. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]] and seats two players. Unlike most other chairs, this item does not offer the ability to [[Chat]] with other players. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was a [[Season Pass]] exclusive item during the Season, available after unlocking the Friendship Tree's second node ({{Cost|36 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Shelf====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a short shelf made of pale wood with two small jars and a folded yellow towel on the lower rack. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|16 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's first node ({{Cost|16 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>

====Spice Rack====
The [[Prop]] offered by this Shop is a wall-mounted spice rack made of pale wood, decorated with three small jars, hanging wooden spoons, and a hanging herb sprig. It is a placeable Prop that can be used to decorate [[Shared Spaces]]. This item is purely cosmetic and does not offer any functionality. When carried on its owner's back, it appears in its original form. It was offered to all players for {{Cost|26 SC|season=Nesting}} during the Season, from the Friendship Tree's third node ({{Cost|62 SC|season=Nesting}} total).<br>
