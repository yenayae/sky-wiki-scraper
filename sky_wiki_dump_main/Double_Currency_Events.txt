This page lists any past or current '''Double Currency events''', such as Double Candle and Double Heart events. During these events, the number of [[Treasure Candles]] in realms, amount of [[Currency#Season Candles|Season Candles]] collected or [[Currency#Hearts|Hearts]] gifted to players is doubled for a certain period of time, usually lasting a week. These events often occur in conjunction with or alongside a [[Seasonal Events|Seasonal Event]] or [[Special Events|Special Event]].

'''The list will be in reverse chronological order, with the most recent at the top. '''
<br>

==Radiance Event==
[[File:Radiance-Event-promo-image-2025.png|left|thumb|180px|Promo Image]]
A new type of event blooms in the realms! From '''Monday, October 13, 2025 at 00:00 PDT (UTC-7)''' through '''Sunday, October 26, 2025 at 23:59 PDT (UTC-7)''', you’ll find that the [[Dyes|Colored Dark Plants]] provide two types of [[Dyes|Colored Light]] more frequently than normal, and across all realms, not just the realms their respective colors typically show up in—which will make it easier to stock up on those dyes more quickly!

Players will be able to collect more black and green dye during the Radiance event and start trading!
